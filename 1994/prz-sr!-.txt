           ---------------------------------------------------
                           introduction.....
           ---------------------------------------------------




 I started making messing around with the concept of making ascii logos
back around April or May, 1993... and they looked like shit, but I still
 continued... as of November that year, I started collecting them.. and
  you will see some of them in the package. This is a collection of my 
  work where many of the items date back as far as October, November of 
1993... therefore, there is a lack of quality here and there.. but I felt
 the need to include them here as they are part of my collection, and a 
     part of me. I hope you enjoy this as much as I did making it..


 Two other things to mention. Firstly, if you at all want me to make a
logo for you, please don't hesitate at all, and ring me up at Biohazard
      Inc, the TRZ CHQ. I'm a co-sysop there, and call a lot... 
  Just drop me mail with what you want, and I'll have it for you in
hours. Oh, and in less than two weeks, we should have a 3 node ringdown
happening... so after much delay... well, we're all excited about this..



 The other thing to mention is that Biohazard is starting the Bio-Net. If
you are interested in joining the fast expanding new network, call us up
and drop Kaptain Kaos a message, and he'll gladly deal with you...



          ...and without further hesitation, the collection...
