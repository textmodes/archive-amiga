Hello, fellow members of Outlaws!

I put this file together so that everyone doesn't have to do the job of
collecting logos, by looking through every new ascii collection coming up on
the boards, etc.. When you're going to release something and need a logo, you
can just browse through this file and quickly pick out the one you like best.
The collection will be updated whenever I have enough logos to add.

If you have any old logos that aren't included, I probably don't have them.
They should be in the next issue if you make sure I get them somehow.


NEWSLETTER?

By the way.. Anyone else but me who thinks it would be nice with some sort of
internal newsletter? Sure the memberlist covers a bit, but it says very little
about what productions are being worked on, what goes on in the crews in the
different countries, and such things.. The mag Asylum (released in January
last year) was very good I think. Too bad it was abandoned. I don't remember
the exact reason for discontinuing the mag, but something similar is certainly
needed. It doesn't have to be any code; just a text file would do fine.
Or what do you think?
