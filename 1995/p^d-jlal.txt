             Dear friends! It's time for me to release my 10th
            collection. Everything has gone so very fast these
            last two months. After having released my first in
              the beginning of July, until this; so much has
                 happened. Good things and ofcoz bad ones.

[-A-historical-pIONEER-dESIGn-production-from-[c-REAL!]------------------]

            After my comeback in the scene, I tried to compare
            the scene today with the one I used to know. And I
             must tell you, there's not much that's the same.

[-A-historical-pIONEER-dESIGn-production-from-[c-REAL!]------------------]

           If one takes a week off nowdays, much happens during
             the time away. Often so much you miss something.
                Especially in this business, the ascii-one.

[-A-historical-pIONEER-dESIGn-production-from-[c-REAL!]------------------]

             Many collections are released during a week. Some
             are quality-releases, but the mainparts are bad.
               Some are so bad the writers should be ashamed
           releasing such colly. I won't give any names, because
          we all think different, but all your writers out there,
            please think twice before releasing something. You
            might regret it later.. I know what it's like to do
                        things without planning...

[-A-historical-pIONEER-dESIGn-production-from-[c-REAL!]------------------]

             Anyway, this collection contains 49 logos from my
           9 previous ones. These are some of my best, according
          to me. If you want some new logos, be sure to download
               this file's twin-file 'Anybody out there'...

[-A-historical-pIONEER-dESIGn-production-from-[c-REAL!]------------------]



                    It's time to go. Bye for now.......




                    Signed: [c-REAL!] / pIONEER dESIGn
