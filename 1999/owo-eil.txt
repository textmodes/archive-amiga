              Welcome Dears! (Is this languageally correct??)

 Anyway,  I want to present you my newest colly in azkee art which  will  be
 live performed at `Error In Line`99` and maybe win the wild compo...
 While I am writing this on Sunday the 28th of march,  one  week  before the
 happening begins,  is the party surely in hot  preparation,  and  also  our
 organisations for the travel to Dresden seems to run  ashore,  or  at least
 goes totally wrong until the last second as it was,   and  propably  always
 will  be,  if I make a journey with Ed,  know as Friedhelm P. in real life,
 a  guy  who loves to sleep or to do anything else until five minutes to the
 start except the stuff that has to be done before...  Not easy to live with
 him!!!
 This  weekend  he  decided  to  stay a few days more in Munich, because his
 parents wanted to visit him. Well, then I have  to  composed  the  tune  we
 wanted  to  write  for  the 4-Channel-Music-Compo on my own, and my friends
 know that I am not a good tracking guy :-))

 Well,  as you maybe noticed pretends this colly to be the 2nd from the `off
 world` colonies, but you think you still haven`t seen anything from us. You
 are  right!  The problem  is,  that  our  planed first one `emergency room`
 hasn`t been finished because Joanne, who has been asked to  write  a  story
 under the  topic,  wasn`t  able to write it in the limited space of time...
 But I guess to wait will be paid back, because this collection  will  be  a
 very special  one.  Not with requested logos. Only ones made for the story.
 Call it an experiment or whatever you want, but  do  not  forget  what  art
 means! Do something different to the others...

 Also I began to work for my 6th colly, the 3rd under the `off world` label,
 called `Visitors`, where I will put some  old  logos  into  I  didn`t  used
 anywhere  before.  Also  I  have to do something in real life for my career
 as author, philosoph and poet, and it seems that I will go ill... DAMNED!!!

 But I have to think positiv with every move I make.  This won`t be hard for
 me these days,  because  the sun is shining and it seems the summer decided
 to start this year a bit earlier! The skirts of girls get shorter everyday,
 they  wear  no  pullovers  anymore (those have hide the good things in live
 the whole winter) and equal if you are thin or  fat  everyone  will  put  a
 smile on you, if you put a smile on him.  Young boys take their cabrios out
 and turn up the volume by crossing through the city streets.  And sometimes
 for a second or so,  if you look out of your window,  your job doesn`t seem
 as hard as it was in the winter...

                                  Enough of introduction, enjoy the logos...

 One year ago, Black Dragon wrote an article he sent me later.  I  felt  the
 same  and  one  year after I decided to include an revided version with new
 imprints of life in scene in my live collection, because I guess a party is
 a good place to show ones opinion about the current state of something...


                            Amiga Kills Itself!!
                             ------------------

 1999 - The new year began with new chances, good or bad.  This year is also
 important for  the  AMIGA,  because  everyone  who can  still call the best
 computer ever his own,  is waiting for some new things. The new OS! And the
 new AMIGA - Next Generation! But equal what QNX and AMIGA Inc.  will  bring
 us,  the   problem is still the same: How to stay on this Computer when you
 see your friends going to a more powerfull  machine???  The  NG`s  are  not
 available at present, so 060 is for most of us the limit.  Maybe some of us
 use 603e+ or similar,  but that is not enough in our world that is ruled by
 speed and also still not standart!!

 Well, it's a choice. If the AMIGA is for you a passion then you will surely
 stay for a while on this platform - you are not interested in others! Let`s
 have a look at C64 or Atari users. They  still  have  computers  whose  are
 really dead - okay, Atari tries it again with the Milan system, but it is a
 question  if  any  other  than Atari users will buy this machine - but they
 love them!! And that is the most important thing!!! Don`t you think so too?
 But most sceners are not supporting our computer with what it  need.  Those
 sceners  are...  Okay,  they  do  great  work with coding marvellous demos,
 painting high quality pictures or composing extraordinary musics.  I  don`t
 want  to  deny  this, but the first thing the AMIGA is in need for are more
 consumers. I am not a guy  who  loves  thinking  capitalistic  but  in  the
 current  world you have to. Especially in technology business. Faster, more
 colorful and cheeper are the rules!! Consumers  brings  interests  for  the
 firms  to  make  more soft- and hardware. Better hardware takes the look on
 the platform from people having other ones. This will make  more  and  more
 people  buying  the  AMIGA,  which  will  bring  interests  for  the  firms
 to make... It`s a closed circle, so you have to start somewhere...   Except
 that  the  things AMIGA Inc. is doing these days - no marketing, only empty
 promises - is ignorance one of the biggest enemys for  the  AMIGA  and  its
 scene. Especially the scenes!!!

 For the business men it's only a question of power.  They are searching for
 a computer more and more powefull for their job.  For  their  business they
 need one with high combatibility to others in the business. So: NO PASSION!
 For the new coming scener - in a false way often called lamer - it's a real
 problem to understand our motivation for the AMIGA.  And  I  can understand
 him!  Because  we are perhaps too `close` with strangers. We have to change
 our reaction on them.
 First thing: We are not really "open"  for  all  contribution  of  sceners.
 Just see the Mags! I think some sceners (new or unknown) try to reach or to
 contribute popular mags with their musics or pictures without success.  The
 doors to the scene seems to be  closed  for  them.  An  imaginary  example:
 A  mag receives a great, stunning, amazing picture done by an artist called
 `NoChance`, but only one day before the mag  will  be  released,  the  main
 editor receives a normal, standart picture by Lazur, Louie,  Ra  or  Facet.
 All of those popular sceners!!!.  What is his choice???  `No Chance`??? No,
 he`s not known!!! It's reality!!! Just see all the mags around and you will
 see the same contributors, and it's the same thing with the musics!!!
 So... your only reaction as newcomer is to find  another  computer  without
 those fences or to resignate and be unknown for the rest of your life!! Ask
 yourself: What would I do in this situation???

 We have to think about one point...  Once, we were also beginners like him.
 So,  it's  time for us to help new sceners to enter our great society.  Our
 computer has the possibilities to pass borders. In  real  life  are  racism
 and killing normal in a strange way.  Not here!!!  Nobody of us is thinking
 bad about another person just because he is not of ones  nationality.  It`s
 equal what the color of your skin is or if you are tall or small.
 So, don't shout when you see a production done by a new group, which is not 
 perfect in your opinion. Those new coming people are  perhaps  the  talents
 for the future AMIGA scene and we have to help them, not to destroy them!!!
 What  about  the next generation? What if the legends and our current chart
 riders are dead one day - or at least out of scene??  Who will continue our
 work, supporting the AMIGA, setting new standarts for code and art???

 So if the AMIGA dies one day, we are also a bit responsible for its death!!
 Don't  think of a danger which comes from another computer, another planet,
 or of too less money and a bad organised industry! Start by having  a  look
 at  yourself!!!  The  problem  is  also  inside  our  scene.  When  we will
 understand this, I think we can start repairing the damages and building up
 a new AMIGA that maybe lasts forever or at least`till the end of mankind...

 In my opinion this article is not antiquated.  Well,  the scene`s situation
 has changed since last year  (luckily for us)  I  got this lines from Black
 Dragon, but there  are  still  ignorance  and  intollerance  against  young
 sceners - beginners.  Think  about while looking at the logos - made by one
 who is not as long in scene as some others...

                                                   Black Dragon`98 / Zito`99
