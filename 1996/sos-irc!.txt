 Not much  special  with this  coolection.  Mostly I  released it  because
 of  all  the  various   requestors  who  had  to  get   their   requests.
 It's all kinda standard,  with the same xCz guarantee as ever which gives
 you the the promise that NEVER to draw style-less, identical ascii logos.

 It's been quite a interesting time since sOS-Mdia.Txt though, I've joined
 HellFire (hF) and  Euphoria (ePH) and I'm  pleased to be one of the lucky
 guys at the #AmiElite chennel on the Irc.

 My old, really  inactive  demo-group  Euphoria  (started 1990) which I've
 been a  part of  since  then  could now be  replaced (or  merged-together
 perhaps) by this  new, active scene-group Euphoria. So, Euphoria (eUP) is
 dead - long live Euphoria (ePH) !

 Most of these requests  were told  to me at the Irc (a place you too will
 have to visit) and that  why I choosed the name  of this production to be
 Ironic, or Ir(c)nic.

 Ironic is also  the name  of the  new single  from  award-winning  singer
 Alanis Morisette, check it out  if you have got the  same taste as me and
 Linda N.S.

 Hope to  see all  of you cool guys in  the scene  at the  #AmiElite soon,
 anyway, that's where I'm to be found...

                 / xClUZiWe of hELLFiRE, eUPHORiA, sPG and sAVE oUR sOULS.
