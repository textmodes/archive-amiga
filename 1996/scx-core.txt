       ascii chars down to earth. nobody moves nobody gets hurt .Yess,
       straight from da bavarian outpost da  gangsta  brings  you  his
       first ascii collection called `Core`. This one is  being  meant
       to be my beginning in da strange  world where  chars  rule  our
       humble beings. this package of flavour contains mainly my works
       of the last two months. nearly all asciis are requests so don`t
       wonder if ya might have already seen some pieces flyin` around.
       so now i leave ya with my first step into  style  and  harmony.
                   enjoy da show and keep up da flavour!

       bout that - next colly will feature mainly  ascii  scene pieces

       notice that is quite a difference between most logos, but  as i
       have told`cha before the asciis had been made in a rather  long
       period (- laaaazyyy) and so i often changed ma own` style.  so,
                   just chill out and don`t fuck it up!
