 much times passed since last colly. thought about a big amiga scene
 respect colly with elend, but he was too busy and i was so too...
 whatever! i did not finish all groups logos anyway at the end, and i
 got finally tired of the scene and did not have time for this kind of
 art, my focus simply changed...           |                   |
                                           |                   |
 luckily poque liked the idea of a co-colly, still keeping the contact
 after more than one year only delaying by me, otherwise this would
 never see the light of day maybe?!        |                   |
                                           |                   |
 some active groups might be missing here, this may be because of two
 reasons: i already made them (several) asciis but they never used them
 (like iris) or i simple forgot about them, about what i am really sorry.
 but as i do have an e-maill addy, just mail me and you will get your
 logo soon!                                |                   |
                                           |                   |
 well, what dipswitch can, we can do it too! ;-) but we do it for the
 real ascii spirit scene, the amiga scene. you may claim it is dead,
 you may claim it is outdated anyway. with this colly including logos
 for active groups only, we show you are damned wrong. go to the parties
 and see what this lovely machines still beats. in style and in hardware
 coding.                                   |                   |
                                           |                   |
 at last one note about the latest tac: it is really good to see it is
 alive again. hold my fingers crossed that this issue was not the last
 one for the next time. and i am happy to read more positive words about
 my stuff than 2 years ago. but i was a bit disappointed too. i know my
 style is not the best and i always wrote that design is not my thing
 (the reason why this colly looks so good is that pogue did the design).
 that again the tac staff should have recognized my activites the last
 year too - i did 5 times more collies than reviewed - but they haven't.
 anyway, i am promising that this colly is the last one featuring this
 ugly triangle style of mine. (thanks for noticing that it is no a
 rippoff!) maybe it is the last ascii-colly ever by me. (i am so f'ed up
 with the nowadays ascii-scene, except some exceptions of course.)
 but if i should do some collection in textbased amiga art one day,
 you will find a totally different style for sure!             |
