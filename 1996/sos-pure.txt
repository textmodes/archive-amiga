 Wise?  Well the question still remains.. Am I that wise after all? =)
        Anyway.. I'll try to do my best putting together the wisest
        words my mind can possibly come up with...

 P.S.S  I'm back, NeBraska that is, with a new collection! This time
        with the funky name of 'Pure, Smooth, Simple '. It's my 4th
        collection overall, this time dedicated to all you sysops out
        there. I know that you are in a never ending quest of trying
        to make your board look as cool as possible, and hopefully
        you'll find some stuff in here to make YOUR board a look a
        little bit diffrent than others..

 Why?   The reason why I put together this collection is because some
        people really liked my last collection (Respect Part 1) but
        unfortunelly there were no logos they could use since all
        the logos in that collection were for the best ascii artist
        around.. Well here you go buds..

 NEWS!  A lot of stuff has happend since I released my last
        collection, some months ago. Just two days afterwards I ran
        into Xcluziwe/sOS who liked my work, so now I'm a proud member
        of SAVE OUR SOULS! I'd like to take this oppertunity to say
        'HaydiHow!' to all sOS members worldwide. You can find me on
        irc in case you need anything..

 mR!?   Mark Ryder is a subject many people have asked me about, and
        what can I say, as many other people, I have a problem with
        Mark Ryder's attitude. The reason for all the rumous is
        probably a text that I released...
        'From Me To You - NeB/sOS vs. mR!/Mo'Soul'
        ..were I said what I thought about the guy in general.
        If Mark Ryder has anything more to say I guess we have to
        keep on fucking up the scene with bull-shit no-good talk!
        Personally I think the matter is dealt with, and I hope
        Mark Ryder will keep his attitude to himself, and then
        that's that...
