Bacdafuckup,

here we go for our next strike in 1997. 
Some of  you might  have  wondered where  I have been  during the  past
months. Well , I was busy with a load of other things . Let's face it :
Who , with his brains  functioning the  way it's supposed  to be spends
his time  sitting  in a  sun-lit  room and  does some  sad attempts  at
Ascii , eh ??? Unless , of course , you live  in Poland , have  neither
friends or a girly and are desperate to release your 1437th collection.
Maybe , if you  have finally  recognized  that you  are  both a fucking
lame-ass and a miserable sucker when it comes to Ascii , you might just
as well release  some text-files . After all , they might be counted as
another colly ...

On to a complete different subject... I would like to welcome  H2o! and
his BBS "Raveland" into ARCLitE. Cool to have you in, Marcus.

With a hideous grin in my  face I have read Mark Ryder's assumption that
ARCLitE is  dead ... plain  fucking  wrong , Mark ... wait  and see  for
yourself , hehehe. Great job on Art Bomb #1 by the way, keep it jammin'.

I really hope that you people out there will  support the mag in any way
you can. After all, it's the first effort done by *artists* and not some
lame waste of time as this RoadCocks - wannabe - jackshit was...
Oh yes, and thanks for all the votes, ehehehe.

Allright , this production contains some old logos as well as a taste of
my new style... in case you have requested a logo , most of them will be
featured in my next release.

There is nothing much  more to write really , so I suggest you lean back
and give in to another piece of ARCLitEnment - RESiStANCE iS fUtiLE...
