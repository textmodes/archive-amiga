 As I already mention  in  `Empty Minds` - the  ones  among  you  who  read
 D.I.S.C.  issue  #11 know  what   I mean - everybody has to make decisions
 from time to time which may change a lot in his life.  Sometimes  they are
 right,  sometimes  they are wrong.  But the way life is,  you almost never
 don`t know this before you make the decision.

 So I had also to decide.  A lot!!!  Scene or Job.  I choose Scene.  Editor
 or Swap. I choose Editor. PKD-Guide or Ascii. I choose PKD-Guide...  I had
 to find the best way for me to solve my problems,  to cope up with my real
 life, to live with my girl, to run my dreams and to stay in scene. Perhaps
 some  of  my  friends  will  be  disappointed  by my decisions, but for me
 this way is the only one at the moment. Maybe I will swap again, make cool
 asciis  and  even  get a nice job when I will have started studying at the
 end of this year. Maybe...
 
 One last statement: I loved ascii-bizz! Ever did although I was no artist.
 I enjoyed ascii-bizz. Really did although I was no  real  artist.  I  will
 surely love it for the rest of my days, but I will be a consumer only from
 now  on.  Some elite guys or other arrogant assholes may be glad to see me
 go, some friends and `fans` will maybe miss my work.  I swear the decision
 was not easy to make! This colly has been planed and started in the middle
 of 2000 when I noticed that I could not cope with the many  things  I  was
 working  on.  Also I tried to re-contact all my contacts through the whole
 last year, but only a few got letters from me!  Really no easy decision to
 be made, believe me.

 And now I want to present you the last ascii-colly of mine. With no style,
 as always.  I  guess  I can surely do from time to time a request for good
 mates but no colly anymore! Also title logos for azkee-artist if they  can
 quarantee for using them...  Tell for what you need the requested logo and
 if you have maximum sizes and so on,  thereby I can create some for you to
 make you happy!
 On the following lines you will find my complete  history  in  ascii-bizz,
 some unreleased logos, my `fonts` and design  plans,  a  small  remix part
 featuring  Incognito,  comments about ascii art  and  some  more  or  less
 interesting extra stuff.

                           Good bye and see you in the next coming diskmag!

                                                 Zito/Darkage^Layout^Reason

 am  crazy,  but  I love too much making ascii art, so I am again accepting
 requests from everybody who needs some logos, pictures or whatever as long
 as it is ascii art.

 But I will not be active as ascii artist in Layout nor join another  ascii
 team. I will only release some guest collies from time to time under other
 ascii lables when I got enough requests. Also I am thinking about some new
 co-productions with friends of mine. But all of this not before I moved to
 Berlin and kicked out the next issue of Showtime. I have too many projects
 running - scene and privat - to be as active in ascii-bizz as I was in `99
 for example.

 At last I would like to mention that there where two more parts planed for
 this colly:  `The  Maniac  Part`  featuring old asciis by me from the very

 ago or so, but all that stuff has been lost due  my  last  three  harddisk
 crashes  at  the  beginning  of  this  year  while  I tried to install new
