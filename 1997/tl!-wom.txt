   Again back after a long break. Just have to wonder how a few artists on
   the scene manage to release quality collections so often. My respects to
    them. I'm a good example of something totally different; I released my
  first collection in 1994, and this one is my 5th or 6th. I hope that I've
   always managed to maintain a high level of design, and that's my goal in
  the future too. It's pathetic to see new artists release low-quality packs
  after another, it's the quality - not the quanity, something that everyone
 knows, but unfortunately seems to forget too often. So I'm hitting under the
     Twilight-label now, we are trying to put some fire under it, and new
    artists are always welcome - if they have the style. If you have some
         material to show us, or some requests / comments, email us.

         Ville aka Sperm Whale / Sin Fame · Twilight / Aurinko / Ruma

       What's happaned to the quality-control? I see good groups taking
         in low-talented artists, and artists respecting someone just
       because 'everyone else does'. Sure it's easy for me to say, i've
      been doing ascii for over three years and have never gotten to one
       of the big groups. That's true but it doesn't change the facts.
       If you respect someone as an ascii-artist, look at his work, not
       the hours he spends on IRC. Many of the guys who have been here
      for many years, are leaving the scene now. Same time, alot of new
     artists are hitting the scene with their PC's. They should be given
       a break also, no one's a maestro when he's born, maybe everyone
     should take a look of the first collections by for example Crusader
      or Red Dee, they aren't much better than the shit most of the new
         artist and I produce. Most of those newbies are real idiots,
  but hey; the talent will always come through, and the shit's gonna be let
   there standing. There's no reason to be arrogant if you aren't provoked.
