       \   his is it. The delayed production that has been lying in my
        \ work-directory for probably about twelve mounths by now. Was
          originally  supposed to be  a new-year  coolection 1997, but
          then my military life came and stopped those plans. Military
          is now, after  more than  ten  mounths over, and  I must say
          that it's a damn relief to finally be done with that. Anyhow
          life as a militarypolice in Stockholm  has given me loads of
          valuable experiences and friends for life. I've been able to
          learn to ride a horse, jump  parashute, do the  beridna hög-
          vakten, take part of  the  annual mp-prawn and tons of other
          stuff in swedens most  variated military-duty. My  sparetime
          during military has been unexisting and I've  had only a few
          days each mounth to catch up with my social life. And that's
          time when  I've been  out  partying, and  done all the other
          things     that      I've       been      missing    aswell.

          This joint is released under sos, hf  and dat labels. I have
          now left  hf, but  I did lot of the work when I was within &
          therefor  didn't  have heart  to  remove  the  whole  intro.

          Save our Souls is today  almost 3 and ¼  year old and  still
          lives on, although we really aint as active as we used to b.
          This production is  number 107 under the sos-label. 1997 has
          been a year of sadness in the  ascii-scene. Inactiveness and
          global lazyness has been ordianry at every large ascii-crew.
          Will this change in 1998? Only the  future could tell, but I
          sure   hope   for   a   rebirth    of    the    ascii-scene.

          Caffeine is what has kept me alive through military and made
          me able to keep on partying  no matter how tired I may be, &
          I felt that it was an  hounorable name for this  production.
          Long live espresso, ristretto,  caffeinepills,  Red Bull and
          irish   coffee,   I    adore   all    of    those    drinks.
