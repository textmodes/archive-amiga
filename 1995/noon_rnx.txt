                 women


                 I've seen lots of them women................
                 i think theres a few i've done..............
                 i'd rather look at those curvy figures......
                 than solve the ties of their twisted minds..

                 No woman for me has ever said...............
                 you better not make that next step..........
                 no woman for me has ever done...............
                 what i didn't have in mind..................

                 Look at the beautiful shapes................
                 makes you wanna do the final phase..........
                 maybe i'll run right up that hill...........
                 the hill is high so i'll increase the pace..

                 Conquering the space is the quest...........
                 bonking the girl has got to be the best.....
                 pick a girl and do her all the day..........
                 and never ever again hear a thing she'll say

                 Saying her praiers she turns to dust........
                 dust you wipe off your hands of lust........
                 i'm ready to find a new space...............
                 what else could i do all these days.........
