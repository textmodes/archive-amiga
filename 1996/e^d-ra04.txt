       have   all  the  artists  gone?  I  haven't  seen  many  nice
       collections lately. Not very active at the moment..  Will  it
       become  active? I guess it's possible. With a collection from
       Stezotehic and another from Vietcong soon to come.  One  from
       Mogue yesterday.

       Surface Spray.. hmm, just another strange name as always.

       If you're active, please do these logo's: Strawberry Delight,
       Epsilon  Design,  Rasta.  (DCC  or  email  them... or in your
       collection)

       I'm  thinking  of  making  an  Epsilon  Design   WWW   page..
       Interesting  idea?  All  the collections could also be there.
       We'll see what happens...

       I'll release this on IRC tonight just like the others, so  if
       you want it you'd better be there... or you could just get it
       from a board I guess, now that I know that these  things  get
       spread far enough.
