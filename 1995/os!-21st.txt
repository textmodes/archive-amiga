year.. so why change now ey?)

next month... so why not visit him then?
Ohh sod off you sad bastards! (but leave the cash!;)

irritate you like hell on those 'special' days!

Ooooohhh jolly, jolly, jolly and more jolly!! It's time for the
holiday-snaps!! ARGHHHHH!!!!

Nope.. i've made up my mind... birthdays suck and to be honest... this
colly might suck even more! ;)
