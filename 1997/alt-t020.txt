--[ Preface ]-------------------------------------------------------------------

(Septemper 27th)

After some time  of silence,  I'm back with a text file  full of signs in random
places. To a very limited amount of viewers the phenomena is also known as ascii
art,  while others would probebly just call it rubbish.  I wonder who's actually
right?

The ascii scene  has really gone  inactive these days.  But there are still some
flooders who thinks  quantity is better  than quality.  Like Mogue. ;) Hehehe...
No, actually I think it's nice to see that someone in Arclite releases something
more than once a year.

If you want to request logos,  write a proper e-mail  to my address which can be
found inside this collection. Stop asking me on IRC, I'll just tell you to write
a mail. And don't expect me to do the logos right away, it can take months! I've
got better things to do than spending all my spare time in front of my computer.



(October 7th)

Happy birthday to Inco,  who turns nineteen years old today,  and to Vouck!, who
turns seventeen. Hip hip hurray, and all that shit.



(October 10th)

Phew!  I've been working night monday to thursday and this weekend I'll work two
times twelve hours. I've got no life!

If you've sent  a letter to my e-mail address  after September 20th  and haven't
got a reply yet,  you'll probably have to wait until the end of October.  That's
because I'm not available  on the net at the moment.  This collection was spread
using Inco's slow toaster.

Just realised that this is my 20th ascii collection. What a waste of time!



(October 17th)

After even more delay, I'm finally releasing this collection. Should've released
it a month ago.  I've already started planning on my next production. Seems like
it will be a respect collection. The only ones who can feel sure to be granted a
logo are the ones in my respect list in this collection.  But I'm thinking about
doing logos for some more artists and groups which I think deserves some respect
too.

-------------------------------------[ Tommy / Altern ^ Spin+ ^ Looker House ]--
