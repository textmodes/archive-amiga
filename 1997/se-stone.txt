 A while since my last release. I lost motivation I lost interest not good.

 Now I return with a little offering I hope you will like. Design has never
 been my strongpoint, and I still claim I suck but here I tried a little to
 make it more bearable for all of ya viewing this collection. This probably
 reflects what I have most wanted to capture in a collection - my disregard
 for people who hate badly designed releases, people who hate large headers
 above each logo, people who hate me and so on.

 There seems to have been an explosion of mediocre artworks in the past few
 months and thats okay. Why? 'Cause it shows a lot of new artists trying to
 impress us with their work. However, *NEVER* assume that you are the best.
 Establishing yourself as someone who deserves respect comes from your work
 and your attitude never from what ya say about yourself. Honours are given
 to those who have earned them, not always the people who want them. Always
 keep this in mind when working on your collection. Always do your best but
 don't do it for the wrong reasons.                                        :
