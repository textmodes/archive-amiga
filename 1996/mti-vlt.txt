        Hello fellow readers.. Once again, after  a  long  wait  and
        inactivity  I  present  my next collection to you. The style
        used in the mainpart was created TODAY and  this  collection
        compiled  TODAY...  and  tomorrow  :) Strange how after some
        months I got inspiration  from  reading  the  Ascii  Gazette
        issue  5  by  Xcluziwe/Sos (great work!) and started dealing
        out cards like this!

        This is a very small collection, but I want to get it out as
        soon  as  possible  to show you that "I'm still here." There
        aren't many requests at this time anyway (guess why).

        So sit back and look at the pretty pictures, and  if  you're
        one  of the lucky four (4) specially selected, you can rip a
        piece of ascii out while your at it..

        I'm looking for an ascii group, so send your offers to me:
