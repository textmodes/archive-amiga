 23.11.2004 - Remember some of those old collections that featured a dude
 calling a bbs as a collection layout? Well, not much joy about bbs's anymore
 so i decided to do this the ftp-site way.

 This is a collection of logos for ftp-sites, drawn in two ways. If it's not
 here, you wont need it ;) I've also included some typical dir names like
 incoming, vcd, pc-iso's and stuff like that.

 ... but why? well, i'm fed up with drawing site layouts over and over again.
 this is the last time for a very long time. Dont come asking because you're
 not going to get a custom made logo set, even for an account in your site..

 Anyways, i've drawn all the logos in two styles and they're all free for you
 all siteops there to use. hope you enjoy them, as much as i enjoy drawing
 ascii :-)

 After this i'm taking a break, so dont expect another colly rel until 2005 ;)
