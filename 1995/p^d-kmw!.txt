                              kEEPING mY wORD

         After have made 9 ascii-collections within two months I'm
          getting quite used to read messages from any different
      persons on my home-systems. Most of them are good reading, but
        there're also those who really have made an effort to make
       me depressed. f.i. We in p^D had choosen a system as our SHQ
       for ascii-releases. The sysop who first was very enthusiastic
        declared only a week after the official announcement, that
         he had had several complains about it. Since I don't know
       who complained, fact is I don't want to know, I won't accuse
          anyone. But I feel that I must get one thing straight:

[-----------------------------------pURE-pIONEER-dESIGN-pRODUCTION-------]

      All you users out there, we in p^D are just some ascii-writers
        with a vision of becoming ever better than we are today. We
        know that we have had some struggles in the past, but to us
                that's over, and we will just look forward.

[-----------------------------------pURE-pIONEER-dESIGN-pRODUCTION-------]

     There are not many people who read all of this text, but I would
       like to make an official appology to 'jUAN/tRSI/iNS/e^D eTC.'
       My welldebated .txt-file (proof that people in Australia read
      it) about our conflict was lame. I didn't realize it then, but
        today when I see whereto it lead, I must say that I'm sorry
           and hope that you can overlook my childish behaviour.

[-----------------------------------pURE-pIONEER-dESIGN-pRODUCTION-------]

       Last, I would like to say, that all you people, please begin
         order ascii from me again. Everything lame that happened
      is history, now in p^D everything is true and clean friendship.

[-----------------------------------pURE-pIONEER-dESIGN-pRODUCTION-------]
