    it's  been a long  time since  i  released  something.  i  was on irc
    constaltly  (perhaps too much)  and  taking  requests and drawing and
    stuff,  but  i  was just sometimes too lazy and sometimes too busy to
    put a design together.  i  have  quite busy months behind me, finally
    finished school  and starting my study  at the  university of cologne
    in october.  till then  i  have  plenty time, so you can expecty much
    releasing    activity    from    me   in    the    next   months...=)

    what's  about  todays  ascii  scene ?   on  the  one hand, i see many
    quality  collies  coming  up.  on  the  other  hand, there was a very
    unpleasant story about  a  pc  ascii  lamer  ripping  mortimer twangs
    ascii art in a very  nasty way. anyway, it's time for a new TAC again
    and  as  soon  i'll finish this colly i'll start working on this one.
    expect  the  whole  program !  reviews, articles, guest artist charts
    and                   the              whole                 stuff...

    now coming to  something  really  sad.  the scene had a very big loss
    last month.  ramone, ascii artist in HOS and LP and musician in MONO,
    passed away  of a sudden heart weakness.   he was a great asciian who
    brought  me  to  astonishment  with his  stylish logos, a magnificent
    musician whose  great jungle  and hiphop  tunes motivated me to track
    myself and a very pleasant  companion  on irc. unfortunately, i never
    managed to meet him  personally, although he lived just like 50k from
    my hometown.  now  i  won't  get  a  chance.  may you REST IN PIECE !
    i           will           always             remember           you.

    now   something   concerning  dtr internal things. we decided to drop
    gravedancer.  since he joined  dtr,  he  didnt  even  show up on irc.
    gravedancer, if you read  this and are really willing to team up with
    us again, you are welcome, but only if you are willing to comtribute.
    same things goes to  spermwhale who joined up with us early this year
    and still didnt show  up.  he's still remainig on our memberlist, but
    spermwhale, if you  won't  show  up and contribute in the next month,
    you'll part the faith of gravedancer.  anyway, i would really like to
    see    you    both    contributing    some     quality     styles   !

    now over to the logos...                                dipswitch/dtr
