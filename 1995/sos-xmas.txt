  .  merry christmas to you all! i was sitting here on christmas eve doing  .
  :  nothing, so i thought i'd finish this old collection i've had lying    :
  .  here for some time now. lots of things has been happening in the       .
  :  ascii scene scince my latest collection, groups die, groups born,      :
  .  guys leave, guys join, people write shit about eachother, people say   .
  :  nice things about eachother, some people release good collections and  :
  .  some release bad. now, you can ask yourself; how many people keep up   .
  :  with everything happening in the asciiscene? let's say that it's       :
  .  about 10 people in sweden. how many of them will leech this colly?     .
  :  and why will they leech it? let's say that 7 people takes a look at    :
  .  this ascii-pack, and that 5 of them only do so because they are        .
  :  searching for desoto-ripps. that will leave us with 2 persons, who     :
  .  reads this because they have had some crazy idea that this collection  .
  :  is good or interesting. to the two of you, i would like to say:        :
  .  - keep on reading. but don't get surprised when the psychiatrist       .
  :  knocks on your door. and to thoose five persons looking for desoto-    :
  .  ripps i would like to say: -keep up the good work! it's guys like you  .
  :  that makes this worth doing!                                           :

  :                                                                         :

  :                                                                         :
  .  as one of 'thoose 10' i have lately discovered the great amount of     .
  :  newbies in the ascii scene, newbies crawling out from under some       :
  .  tiny rock, using some half-ripped mega-boring asciistyle and thinking  .
  :  they owned the scene or something. of course, i don't say that every   :
  .  beginner is lousy, there are some with a great talent too. but a very  .
  :  high amount of those 'my first collection' i read is of a low quality. :
  .  it almost seem like some guys are so bored that they just have to      .
  :  make this ascii, and i don't think there is something wrong with just  :
  .  sitting infront of their computers making asciis for their friends.    .
  :  but why do they have to release it? is it because they all want to     :
  .  make themselves a reputation as a famous ascii writer, and become a    .
  :  part of this giant 'ascii-scene'? that is in my oppinion the correct   :
  .  answer of this question. now you might ask yourself; shall we let      .
  :  those 'newbies' in to the ascii-scene? can anyone be a part of the     :
  .  ascii scene? when do you become a part of it? who are a part of it?    .
  :  do you have to be in a group to be a part of it? am i a part of the    :
  .  ascii scene? are you? ... many thoughts cross my mind as a sit here,   .
