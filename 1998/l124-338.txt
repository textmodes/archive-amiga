hi  there  one more time.  this little piece of ascii is not an normall ascii
collection.   it`s  published  under  link124  tag, it`s amother one official
release,  but  don`t  treat this as normal well designed and ful of requested
asciis  collection.   "rebirth  of  stylism" is an continuation of my previos
styles  collection  named  "styles  retrospection".  it`s an storage of ascii
work  styles  done  by  me  during those over two and a half of year in sweet
ascii bussiness - from the beggining of july 1995 to april 1998 - 72 (seventy
two)  styles and visible evolution of my skills...  it`s only 122 ascii logos
selected  from  over 3500 i`ve done till today.  also i dne a lot of adverts,
votesheets,  letter forms for scene swappers, board design for madbart`s "the
sect bbs" and so much more others.  completely ascii servce, eheh =)
                          |                       |
this  piece  of  history  closed  in one ascii file is plitted to two parts -
first  is  the  ctp`s  era.  ctp (counterpoint) was my previous handle on the
scene  (till  july`97).  later i`ve changed it into azzaro.  so for you all -
ctp and azzaro are the same person.  update your notes now.
                          |                       |
my  ascii carreer begins on intel outside party 2 when i publish my first own
collection  named  "ozone friendly".  when i look at it today i think it`s so
shitty,  but it was my first step into this magic land...  later after half a
year of being independent ascii maker me and seti formed ascii crew named amf
(ascii  makers  federation).  it kicks from january`96 to november`96.  later
we  change  the  name  to link124 and it`s still alive and kicking.  novadays
link124 is full of tallented polish and one german makers aka.  acid, chosen,
budgie,  revi,  covenant  and  me.   there was a couple of other members like
seti,  sleeper  and  nemes!s which joins us and left but i think link124 is a
stable and leading polish ascii group.  it`s over 40 releases in 18 months of
existing  and  still comes new ones.  we are productive and fast - every week
you  could  watch  new  ascii  collection  done  by  us.   a  lot of them are
co-opertions  between members - it`s cool and two times better =) during some
troubles we were the only one team in poland, but it seems to new force comes
-  linear  (la!-abcd.txt)  is born and old doggystyle seems to be still alive
while  sundance  last  releases.   time  goes  on,  so if you want to see how
skilled  or  how lame am i - watch this "collection" and request new logos in
one of your favourite styles.  contact me and don`t hesistate to spread those
link124 releases (l124-abc.txt) across the boards.  thank you and have a nice
day.   if  you  want to join us - send samples by snail or e-mail to our [hq]
and wait for a reply.     |                       |
