light... 'the ledge'  in the song   was  a  huge  low hanging  tree branch on a
big  old  oak tree where  Richard    and  his  pals would congregate on to play
(and climb)  when they were little kids... as well as being  a wonderful bit of
music + lyrics (especially for a 17yr old!) it also totally  sums up how i have
been  feeling   the   last few weeks...
