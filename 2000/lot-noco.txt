    ...once we lived in peace! well, not in real peace. man was always to
  fight against each other if he hadn`t had anything else to fight against.
           but at least we could have lived in peace with our `selfs`.
 we were sure of the values we had. the things we called our own. house,
           pets, country, job, wife. modern times changed everything.
     man learned how to use his brain - a bad invention! besides developing
      better weapons than the broken part of a tree - a weapon he found
    only by accident - we drove into the deeper levels of our own mind
                                and discovered the other side in us.
                      for a lot it was a rather good experience.
             they discovered a spirit of health and goodness.
   some only thought that they discovered the good inside.
      but equal what the good side finally was, others discovered
  the black holes of their own identities. the mirror of what they thought
         to be. some were shocked or even more than shocked,
    but others were fascinated by there own badness.
       and while the people - who discovered how to help - tried to do their
          best to make a better world of the places we were living in,
             the bad guys began to develope their devlish things
                           ·                and started their evil game...

                     "were will we go???", somebody`s soul is asking.
   "may there be those bad guys, with their bad thoughts, from their bad
              minds, behind bad faces, making their bad plans.
                      there must be enough of the good ones, don`t they?"
  sure there are a lot of them, but they - not knowing what they are doing -
          are helping the evil ones in a way they call science...
    developing, or at least trying to develope, things which shall help
            the human race through its way of life, the `scientists` always
        discovered that not everything is going as they awaited it to go.
                experiments went wrong and out came a monster,
                   a thing for destroying, a bomb, a black hole.
   and while we try to make our way to the universe, we forgot to preserve
       our wrong experiences and the bad guys get them for their use.
        we were irresponsible. a fault which may bring us to death.
                                               some day.
   I know some guys who always knew this before the main of us.
    they could imagine what probably happens, but as it always is:
                                                 no one believed them.
     "they are clowns. idiots. man without brains. just sci-fi authors..."
          but today?! remember what they wrote about nuclear bombs.
    the events connected to cold war. russians winning by destroying
       america and making the whole world to their slaves. or americans
                 killing mankind by using their atomic bombs.
   the nuclear fallout after a crash of another challenger rocket.
       deadly winds of the sun. everything we laughed about...
                                    we never believed...
     those `shitty` ideas like androids or little robots faking the murder
               on a person. we always thought that this can`t be true.
                            that this was only an idea of an idot.
                   but we never had an eye on the science.
   what intelligent man invented is no longer fiction.
                                  it became reality. a scaring reality...

    besides the things everyone already have knowledge of like bombs,
 atomic energy, rockets, viruses... (and I could continue) mankind developed
   something very new. something really ass-kicking.
                                    I discovered this new invention,
       which shall be soooooo good and helpful for every people living
      on earth, in a well-known magazin of science. and later I saw a short
    documentation about it on one of my several tv-chanels. and I wouldn`t
     believe it. hmh, at first I only watched it and said as every modern
               human kind of being: "oh, that`s nice and maybe helpful."
  it was a thing that seemed so normal today and doesn`t shock anyone who
   gets to know of it because we are already in a mood to accept those new
    things... productions. more and more with everyday it seems no problem
     for us to cope with new unbelievable and incomprehensible things.
 every new invention seems to be something we need. or at least could need
                       sometimes in the future. everything IS so normal...
   therfore I didn`t notice how cruel this new idea was at the first sight.
 living in a world where robots as small as atoms, which can be placed in
    a human body without his knowledge, you could easily run into a trap you
                                          never thought to be one.
              so it happened to me. I thought it could be good too.
      but it isn`t! mankind only moved from microchips to nanotechnology...
                       anyway, in this article I read about scientists
   in the united states of our beloved and great america who cooperated
                              with scientist at a german university.
    the americans had an idea they thought to share with one of the best
      biomechanic labors in the world, the "max planck institute".
        the thing was secret until the middle of 1999, but now they are in
          a new phase, so they showed it to the public.
         we, the rest of the mankind, has been informed about
        the american scientists who are working on a programm,
       that saves every information of brain activity.
 the speed of information exchange, where the brain "thinks" what to do,
    realization of neutral inputs. the scientists in germany meanwhile
   are on the way to develope computer chips which are able to be controled
  by biologic matter. means nanochips that trade information by using
                organic substance or even control it by electric impulses.
                                             imagine,
 if both experiments will success and join together their information, they
    will create a chip which is able to communicate even with brain-cells.
     not only to trade information, but to exhange commands. in this way,
    it becomes a chip which will not only help makind by recognizing for
     example written words in a faster way, but a chip which could be
    programmed to control your flesh and muscles! and here we go from
   nanotechnology to cyberimplants. what in sf-books was a neccessary tool
  to make a real sf-stories becomes true, but ask yourself one question:
                              is this really indispensable?
