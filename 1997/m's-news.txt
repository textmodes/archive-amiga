 ::: ::: while youre pumping some warez to several boards at the same time!

 :::.::: really think where has all the friendship gone? On #amielite if you

 ::: ::: when they need help with something, compared to known guys who get
 :::.::: help right away if needed! Ive felt this myself as i sometimes use
