             I'm back shortly after my coop with Stezotehic/Style!
             I was doing this (My Reflection) at the time me & sTZ
             decided to make a coop so I compiled this  one   now.
             Ofcourse the logos which I planed to have in this one
             got into the coop colly and this is just the rest  of
             the requests. I made these requests VERY quickly just
             to get rid of them heh. So now I don't have a  single
             request waiting to be done. Pretty reliefing really..
             Maybe I'll start compiling a tribute collection. Tell
             me if you wish to see one !!!.. Better get those resp
             ects coming if you wish to see your logo in it! heh..

             Spring's knocking on the door and the snow is melting
             really fast. It's about time really since it's been a
             hellofa winter. It sure might be that I wont be doing
             any damn ascii for a while... Oh yeah, I suppose this           .
             is my 16th collection ever. 13th for Jedi Arts.
