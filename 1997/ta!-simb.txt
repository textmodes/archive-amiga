 : i  usually don't write much  and at least not for  a preface  of an ascii .
 · colly,  but this time  i  have some important thoughts to share with you. ·
 : could  this  be  the first time  that  ascii collection's  preface  tells :
 | something else than bullshit? not, but read on.                           |
 | i gave many thoughts  for the matter  whether i should release this colly |
 | as independent or under the tropic!ascii label.  then i thought, what the |
 · hell,  it isn't my fault that tA! has gotten such a bad name and the best |
 . way to clear  our reputation  is to  make quality  (this thing  is you to |
 : decide) ascii collections and forget the shit which we've been thru. also |
 | releasing this collection independent  would be quitting and i never want |
 | to be a quitter.                                                          :
 | after i joined tA!,  venturus decided to leave us  to become independent. .
 | that  was  a big loss  for us  because venturus  was and is  a  damn good :
 : artist so i hope people against us are happy now.  venturus told me after ·
 | his leaving from tA!  that the main reason  for him  to leave us  was our :
 | so called  bad reputation.  do you think  that it's  so strange  to be an |
 | ascii artist  who  uses a pc  for designing? because our reputation can't |
 | be so bad  cause of that board thing with zinko.  isn't the main point of |
 | this ascii business  to  design  good ascii  and  enjoy  and  not to drag |
 | someone else's reputation  to the dirt?  people, give it  a thought while |
 | watching this collection. i hope that i didn't offend anyone.             |
 | that's it.  now i'll head on to the other matters like  contact info. you .
 : can send ascii requests, thoughts about the text above or almost anything :
 . to my email  (daeron@plazma.net)  or you can find me on some boards  like |
 : the killing playground, freakout,  toyfactory, hardtarget, dragonlance or |
 | some tropic!ascii boards. i also hang out on irc a lot on #ascii.         |
 | and yes,  i  do know that my english sucks like hell,  but i have managed :
 | to live with it.                                                          |
