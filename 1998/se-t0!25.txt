    Preface ----------------------------------------------------------------

    After five years and twentyfive ascii collections I'm still playing ball
    (no, I'm not actually playing ball,  it's a figure of speech you idiot).
    Has it been worth all the time I've spent? Both yes and no. It's quite a
    waste of time,  really.  I mean,  hardly anyone  sees your art nowadays.
    Especially  since there ain't  much left  of the  BBS scene.  But on the
    other  hand  making ascii  is a  good way  of improving  your  designing
    skills.  You have a limited amount of characters  which you have to make
    as much as possible with. So it hasn't been a total waste of time.

    If you  didn't know  I'm actually  a musician.   I started  making music
    before I started making ascii. And I'll never quit making music. It will
    be a huge  part of my life  as long as I live.  If you want  to hear all
    the  modules  I've ever  released  (including  all the  junk)  check out
    ftp.noice.org/pub/incoming  or something.  Respects  to those  who still
    makes chipmusic.
