          ____________________________|______________________________
           After four asciicollections released within a month, I've
           decided to slow down a bit. Though it's nice to see own
           productions on various systems, ones name can be taken
           unserious due to the many releases. Some might think that
           my logos are make without any thinking, or sense of
           logo-bility, which isn't true. Behind a ascii-collection
           like this lies many hours of hard work.
           ___________________________|_____________________________
           The concept which laid way for this collection was deeply
           thought-over before turned into letters. But I don't
           think that anyone have make a collection only containing
           greetings to persons they respect, love, and have as good
           friends. I have now. I've also tried to make the asciis
           as plain as possible. I think it looks great. The purpose
           of my work is not only to be best, but also being
           different and be the first to inspire other great 
           ascii-writers to do things they didn't have any ideas of
           doing only some weeks ago.
           ___________________________|_____________________________
           One thing I've started, which have been quite noticed is
           the fact that I divide my collections in different
           editions. One testcollection has been sent away to the
           U.S, but when this text's being written, I haven't got
           any response.
           ___________________________|_____________________________
           Okay folks. Time has come to enter a whole new collection
           from me, made with style and finess. I'll take some weeks
           off the ascii-scene, but meanwhile you can check out my
           modules poping up on systems with module-areas. Be sure
           to check them out. And just one more thing: 
          
           "Do believe the hype; [c-REAL!] rules".
