     : Its time for you to read a few lines before you can turn off   :
     : the lights and enjoy the show! As most guys already know im    :
     : back in the reborn MO'SOUL again, i here have to thank the     :
     : asciigroups who offered me a place in their memberlist, it     :
     : feels nice to know that people still like my work! But for me  :
     : there never was a thought of joining any other groups besides  :
     : MO'SOUL, as this is where i have my real friends from the      :
     : asciiscene + that they are good artists makes it even better!  :
     : I did release one collection during the time M'S had its little:
     : "break" eventhough i knew that we were coming back, i made my  :
     : first ever co-op with Taxi, who imho is an underrated artist,  :
     : who should get more attention of his art.                      :
     : Anyways its good to be back with my friends again, eventho i do:
     : miss B, DESOTO and VINZI from the old gang! :) B/NUP still roxx:
     : in his PRO^ARTS, hope you like your stay there m8! DESOTO/VINZI:
     : both seems to have lost the intrest in ascii's while, DESOTO is:
     : is concentrating more on real gfx, and from what art ive seen  :
     : from him i can only say that hes good!                         :
     : While talking about art, i just burned myself a copy of ALL my :
     : scenefiles from my amiga, including 650mb mods/sids/gfx/ascii  :
     : with all my asciicollections that ive been collecting, in total:
     : over 2000collies from 93' and to todays.. *ROXX* :-)           :
     :                                                                :
     : Ive gotten myself a PC just like the rest of the M'S gang, but :
     : i still have my amiga left which i boot every now and then just:
     : to do an ascii or two. Since ascii is easily made on amiga, i  :
     : havent seen ANY good asciieditors on pc that can be compared to:
     : the good old Cygnus Editor on Amiga!                           :
     : Now you expect me to say amiga is lame/crap/shit because i have:
     : bought a pc, right? Well i only have to say that todays scene- :
     : people are talking too much about friendship and all that, but :
     : when you see the reality, you realise that you got backstabbers:
     : within the guys you thought were your friends! Seeing groups   :
     : dying, members beeing kicked, guys leaving joining new groups  :
     : etc, that quite much shows the spirit of many groups on the    :
     : scene today! The lack of friendship WITHIN the groups!         :
     : I have myself gotten bored of all this and im not doing much   :
     : work for the scene right now. I have stopped most of my trading:
     : activities and so have CANDYMAN, we will only trade HF releases:
     : and leave the rest to CASE to trade and let her continue to    :
     : *KILL* all the fun and competition that there once was!!       :
     :                                                                :
     : From now on i will continue dealing with HELLFIRE internal     :
     : organising along with the rest of the staff. And just having   :
     : fun with the real friends on this scene who i know i can trust,:
     : without having any need to worry about getting stabbed in the  :
     : back as soon as i turn around!                                 :
     :                                                                :
     : Anyways.. This year me and my brother CANDYMAN will most likely:
     : be attending at THE PARTY 8 in Denmark again, hopefully well be:
     : meeting you guys there who know us and we will have some beers!:
     : And if the STYLE gang comes this year aswell, i might join in  :
     : on a new "STYLE DOES IT LIVE" colly! :)                        :
     : So guys come and visit C&C if you come to tp8!                 :
     :                                                                :
     : After this colly there will propably take sometime till i      :
     : release a new colly, as i feel like i dont have that much time :
     : or motivation for doing ascii's. But that doesnt mean i quit   :
     : doing ascii's, i just calm down abit with the requests and all :
     : that and concentrate on enjoying the scene without any pressure:
     : and just enjoy beeing with my FRIENDS in the groups that are   :
     : standing closest to my heart, namely MO'SOUL and HELLFIRE!!    :
     :                                                                :
