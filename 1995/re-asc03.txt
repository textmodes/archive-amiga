       all nasty rumors about me circling on some systems. After

       and users, that many of you users have got the wrong idea of
       me as a person. Therefor, I decided that it's about time
       to reveal the truth about myself. Just to stop all rumors.

          in them, will solve any problems.

          what some dark elements may try to say.
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 
       -> I'm no PD-swapper. Actually I don't see myself as a
          swapper, but a user. I use both elite- and PD- systems
          to get what I need to my computer, but I never dl/ul
          across the line.

       -> I'm not against piracy. Not completly. It's illegal,
          I know, but I think that most of the "problems" with
          piracy could be solved if all software-houses lowered
          their prices. I think that piracy is a good way to make
          them realize that they could gain more profit by lower
          them. That's my opinion.

       -> I'm not a member of ANY group. Not yet! I've got some
          offers, and ok, I've said maybe to one, but nothing's

          just two groups helping me to release my productions.

       have been changed since 'real pleasure'. I'll continue doing
