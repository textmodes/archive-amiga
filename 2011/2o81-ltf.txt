                    |          the idea behind          :
    

  by accident (& the evil spot/up rough) brought back to the amiga ascii scene
   in 2010 zito - formely under 1oo%, layout, scarab etc. - realized that the
       only missing thing at the great ascii portal asciiarena.com is the
   possibility to download all collies by a group or individual (or even the
  whole asciiarena in case of an oldskool hd-crash) with one click instead of
               digging through this otherwise amazing database.
 so when he was checking his own archives and some cds lying around in his amiga
 floppy boxes and crawling the internet for some more collies he could upload at
   asciiarena to gain some credits and even become one of the top5uploaders, he
    got his hands on a cd named "legends #1 - amiga ascii cd" done by contra!
 wayback in time. and he said to himself: great idea! lets put this to nowadays
        standards by updating, dupe-checking, add-strapping and so on.
 
   and here we are going to get an archive downloadabel by one click (or you
  can order by snailmail a cd/dvd if you like) with all the collies online at
   asciiarena PLUS other internet sources plus private & old scene sources to
   make the mightiest & most complete amiga ascii collections collection for
                      you available in the new millenium.
 
      we hope you like the idea & the way we started it. drop us a mail at
   asciiarena or any bbs alive these days to tell us what you think, what you
  like & what you like to become better or even to send us a colly even we do
                            not have included yet.
 
                     zito & nemo nobody on behalf of 2081
