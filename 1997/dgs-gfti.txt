   Today,  after some time of silent, I proudly present my latest collection.
It's  name's  ofkoz  'Graffiti'.   Why 'Graffiti'?  Beacause!  :) I like very
much  this  kind  of  art,  so  I've called its 'Graffiti'.  Easy.  I hope my
asciis  seem  to  be real gf!  art.  8) These guys, who feel the power of it,
will understand me, I guess.  Nevermind.  Graffiti rulla and basta!

   Today  is 3o.o5.1997.  Fuck.  This collection should be released long time
ago,  about  last  week  of march.  Unfortunately my harddisk drive died.  :(
Yes.   I  lost  ALL  my asciis, collies, demos, mods, pictures...  ALL stuff.
I'm very depressed 'til now, although this event took a place 3 weeks ago.  I
still  feel upset, very upset...  Version 1.0 of this collection was ready to
spreading!   Fuck!   Fuck!   Fuck!  As you can see, I've made 'Graffiti' once
again.  I had to do it.  Ascii is my life.  This is my way out...  But I felt
also  something  else...  something bad.  I was tired and I didn't want to do
ascii anymore.  I know some mates thinks at this moment, that this collection
is  my  last  one.   And  I'm dead as an ascii maker.  No.  I'm going to make
asciis all the time.  :) I can't drop it.  The only change in my ascii career
is  leaving Doggystyle.  Yes.  DGS will be dead soon.  8( I do it, beacause I
haven't  a  modem.   I  stopped  my  swapping  activity,  so I couldn't be an
organizer  of any group.  Now I'm a graphician and I have no time to do other
things  except painting and asciing.  What am I going to join?  It's my sweet
mystery.  ;)

                                                   Acid/Doggystyle
