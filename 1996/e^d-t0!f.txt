What's that  bullshit about  last collection?  Well,  I'm not leaving  the ascii
scene,  if that's what you thought.  I'm just leaving Epsilon Design.  Why,  you
might ask?  Simply because I don't think it's fun to be a member of e^D anymore.
The group has too many members and no spirit.  So I've decided to leave. Maybe I
should have waited a while to see what's  happening since Fatal recently decided
to make an effort to put the spirit back. But I really see no use. It's time for
me to move on.  So right now  I'm not a  member of any  ascii group.  Offers are
welcome.
Nowadays I don't  have much time left  for the scene at all  since my university
studies takes most of it.  So if you want me to make a logo for you, write me an
e-mail and  ask nicely.  And state what  it will be used for,  so I know how the
design should look like, or more how it should not look like.  Then I might do a
logo for you... Maybe.
That's it from me for now, enjoy my 15th mistake.                        - Tommy
