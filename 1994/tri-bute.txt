  This is my 2nd ASCii collection.  The first one got a pretty good 

response from some people.  This will probably be my last collection ever.

Most of you will probably be thankful for that.

  Before we get on with this collecion I have a few words I would like to

say.  First off, I think it is pretty ridiculous how most of the ASCii

painters now seem to put in 20 blank lines or all this 
                             ________________ 
                              _______________)
                             (_____________ etc.

shit in there collectiions just to get them up to 100k and more...

I personally hate tryign to read a collection with all this bullshit in it,

there is no reason for it and making these squiggly things takes *NO* talent

at all.

  Secondly I would like to make this *VERY* clear.  ASCii painters work

VERY hard on their collections.  And because of this, the artists signature

should NEVER be removed, even if you cut and paste letters or change the 

stylin' a little bit... I did have this happen to me by Deathwish of Deep

Freeze, and it was the lamest thing cuz he denied it 1oo%.  Told me he made

the style himself... but anyone who was on Deep Freeze and looks at this

collection, pay close attention to what my first colly had in it... it will

look very familiar, except Deathwish changed my Y's to V's.. and he 

removed the +TRi- signature on the logo.  If you use anything from this 

collection, or any other artists collection, LEAVE THE SIGNATURES ON!

  Well, now that this is off my chest, i urge you to proceed and check out 

the collection.  If you are wondering what style I am currently painting, I

am now painting with the style used in the 'PROJECT UNKNOWN', 'AGA/CD32', 

'CHAOS THEORY', and 'EAGLE SOFT'.  So if you order anything from me, you

will egt this style unless you ask for the older styles or an original

style... well, enough babbling... gaze on...

F · o · R · e · W · o · R · D -
~ ^ ~ ^ ~ ^ ~ ^ ~ ^ ~ ^ ~ ^ ~ 

  For me this is one of the most important parts of this collection because

it brings back memories.  I dunno if it will for any of you, but i hope it 

does.  Memories of the days when friendship was 1oo% REAL.  No one charged

for CC's and codes.  No groups made thier BBS's pay for the support they 

gave to them.  I dunno how many of you have been around long enough to

remember the FBI's Operation Sundevil in the USA, which proved to be the

downfall of FBR.  This section is dedicated to what I consider to be the

original heros... the guys who started the scene and all it is.  The groups

who will forever be immortalized in my mind and hopefully in the minds of 

many others.  If you missed these days and have never heard of these groups

you have missed the glory years of Commodore, and i suggest you go out and

find someone and ask them about these days past.  I guarantee that they 

will have stories that you may not believe...
