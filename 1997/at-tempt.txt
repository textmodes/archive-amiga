Old folks use to say that you never can get rid of the shit. Indeed. It's me,
Venturus on the keys again trying to type some crap to this preface. Again,
I wonder why in the hell these prefaces and intro texts have become a part of
ascii collections.. Who reads these anyways? Blah. Like I would even care.

As you probably have already found out, I joined to Alien Technology, a cute
new ascii group founded by Behemoth.. I am quite sure that I'm going to enjoy
my staying in /\t. In my opinion, despite /\t is a new group, it has quite
good potential. meissä on munaa - we've got the balls, you squeek.

seriously time to get veeery drunk. Again. I just cannot wait to get the first
gulp of the king of the spirits, Pohjan Poika. Ave! A bit of preview now?

The ascii scene has become quiet these days. Too quiet. Is this form of
computer art dying? I just hope it's just temporary inactivity what we are
talking about.. One thing is, that some artists have quitted drawing because
they have sold their amys and bought a PC. Or at least they claim that it is
the reason for quitting. I think that it is a stupid excuse for leaving the
ascii scene. Why don't you just say "I'm really tired to this shit, I have
a real life now, so long, suckers" if you really mean that.

This may sound pathetic but PC is not that bad after all. Of course, there
are things you cannot do with a PC well but there are also lots of cool stuff
you cannot do without having one. After all, the only things which really
suck are your attitudes and prejucides ;) And I know that Windoze sucks,
Microsoft is worse than hell etc. but hey, if you hate Windoze, why don't you
try OS/2 or Linux? At least they work and multitask :)

There's an old finnish proverb that says


     "YOU CAN GET USED TO EVERYTHING.. ..EXCEPT AN ICICLE IN YOUR ASSHOLE
                BUT LUCKILY, IT WILL MELT AWAY AFTER A WHILE"


and another that says


                    "YOU SHOULD TRY EVERYTHING ONCE EXCEPT
                 FUCKING YOUR PARENTS OR DANCING FOLK DANCES"


Got the point? :) And if you don't get along with Aciddraw, QEdit or the
others, let UAE (68020 emulator) and CygnusED do the thing :)

Blah.. This has been more or less stupid propaganda, but I guess nobody really
cares what "a stupid peecee lamer" is trying to babble.
