 welcome to "as the dust settles", my first ascii collection since 2003. dating
 back to 1995 i  have managed  at least one  colly per year. although  like the
 majority of ascii artists  nowadays, i`ve grown out  of my teens and ascii has   taken a  step back in my life. however, the desire somehow  still  exists from
 time to  time to continue with this crazyness. design wise, i admit this colly
 isnt exactly the highest quality i`ve done. using notepad and a pc in place of   ced still hasnt settled with me. (my excuse)

 as some of  you may have  noticed, i`ve stepped down  as aerosol organiser and
 my good  friend  dipswitch  has  taken the  reigns. originally, together  with 
 blue and karlos we decided to leave aerosol and the ascii scene. although it`s
 been  decided  to  remain  in the  group. we  all  wish  dipswitch  a long and
 successful  time with  keeping aerosol  in the game. over  the last few months
 vouck left to re-form his  own group arid weed and on a  positive note, french
 newcomer nerv has joined our ranks and recently released his debut.
 
 midway through 9 years , aerosol still look strong and will  be here  for some
 time  to come. so  until my next  collection, whenever  that maybe  enjoy  the 
 logos below and feel free to get in contact with me for requests.
