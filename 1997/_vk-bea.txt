      - s T O R Y -

 One afternoon a big wolf waited in a dark forest for a little girl to
 come along carrying a basket of food to her grandmother. Finally a little
 girl did come along and she was carrying a basket of food. 'Are you
 carrying that basket to your grandmother?' asked the wolf. The little
 girl said yes, she was. So the wolf asked her where the grandmother lived
 and the little girl told him and he disappeared into the wood.
  When the little girl opened the door of her grandmother's house she
 saw that there was somebody in bed with a nightcap and nightgown on.
 She had approached no nearer than twenty-five feet from the bed when
 she saw that it was not her grandmother but the wolf, for even in a
 nightcap a wolf does not look any more like your grandmother than the
 Metro-Goldwyn lion looks like Calvin Coolidge. So the girl took an automatic
 out of her basket shot the wolf dead.

                                             James Thurber
