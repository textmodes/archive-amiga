     :   I sit here lighting yet another cigarette and thinking what the
     :  fuck:to write Every time this happens nothing comes to mind and I
     :    sit there like some dumb bastard waiting for the inspiration.
     :  All the logos and design are finished and yet I can't even write
     :      a few words for the introduction. This is something of a
.....:    renaissance for me in terms of quality. A few chats with the
:    :  undeniably modest Mark Ryder, the cute but muscular Crusader, and
: ...:   a good look at some of the work coming from the so-called "new
:.:.:   school" of ascii artists had led me to work a little harder on my
  :...    productions. Look somewhere in here for my respects to those
     :      :           that influenced this collection.
     :      :
     :  It never ceases to amaze me, the attitude of some artists on the
     :   scene. They attack others for being arrogant or lazy (sometimes
     :    both) and yet if you look at their own work it never seems to
     :   stand out from any of the other collections that pass by every
     :   day, while at the same time some of the best releases each year
     :  come:from guys who quietly work away and don't look for people to
     :   say:"great work" but do it from a sense of enjoyment. Every day
     .  for me it becomes harder to sit down in front of the keyboard. I
           have finished my 4 year degree course, and as I do this, I
     .   suddenly realise that the real world is just around the corner.
     .  No more sitting up until stupid hours of the morning in front of
     :  the computer, but instead I will have to start putting money away
     :   for a mortgage and bills, who knows, maybe even a family in the
     :  next:few years. Thoughts like this scare me and I feel comforted
     :     by those around me who have been through it, or have yet to
     :  approach this level of life. It becomes weirder every day when I
     :  still consider myself so young, but my life slips away every day
     :      :      just a little further out of my own reach.
     :      :
     :  On the rare occasions I do write a big introduction I like to try
     :    and give a little insight as to my own life. A weird thing I
     :  guess. Telling all the people who read my collections about me.
     :     How many of you will ever meet me in the flesh? Not many I
     :   guess, but still a lot of you who download this collection will
     :   read all this meaningless crap. That in itself is a statement I
     :      :     think, but I have no idea what it represents.
     :      :
     :   I took the time to read through some old Arclite collections I
     :  had on my board a few days ago, and damn. All the old guys who I
     :   respected (and sometimes even chatted to) have disappeared from
     :   the:scene. I talked to Exocet and Green Eyed Monster many years
     :    ago on Point Blank in the US. I had just started out drawing
     :  ascii properly back then, and Exocet actually drew a logo for me
     :   (it:said "Entity" and was released in a collection by him but I
     :   can't remember the name of it), so did G.E.M (saying "Defekt")
     :   and:I couldn't believe that a no-hoper scene novice like myself
     :    could get logos from these guys I idolised. It was incredible
     :    (sorry if you're blushing Rainer :). But now I look round and
     :   see:so few of them, and the artists who are there seem to do so
     :  little. That might be for many reasons but it feels like part of
     :   the:ascii scene tapestry I looked at every day is fading away.
     :    New players move in and the old ones move out. Where do I fit
     :   in?:I guess that is for you all to decide. It would be the most
     :   monstrous of men who claimed their own fame above their ability
     :    without even consulting the opinions of another. Think:about
     :    that, it's probably the most sensible thing I will ever say.
     :      :
     :     Something that struck me hard was the fact that Karma would
     :   always talk about his real life, something that so many people
     :  don't openly share any more. His soul was there for thousands to
     :   read and yet most of us never met him. What a strange thought,
     :   and:now I find myself running along the same lines. That is not
     :      :           a comparison but an observation.
     :      :
     :     Someone once asked me which was my favourite collection by
     :     myself. I sat long and hard, even looked back at what I had
     :   released and still couldn't answer, so I asked them a question:
.  ..:......:.. . .
  ...:........ .   "Do you mean quality-wise or memory-wise?"
     :      :
     :     They didn't understand me, so let me explain that a little.
     :   Every collection I have released, I can tell you a little story
     :      leading up to it. Maybe not scene related, or maybe just
     :     something funny a friend did the day before. At the time of
     :    writing this one, my flatmate and his girlfriend (my ex-girl)
     :  just:came into my room and told me that they are getting engaged.
     :    That was fucking weird for me. But it's an experience that is
     :     forever linked with this collection. Maybe a little sad and
     :romantic for those cold hearts amongst you to grip, but that's life.
     :      :
     :   The:more time I have on my hands, the less things I have to do
     :      with it all. I'm a single man with a bad case of nicotine
     :   addiction, I'm only 22 but already my life is planned by myself
     :    and there seems nothing left for me to do. My options? I have
     :      :       only one, I must embrace the darkness ...
     :      :

These messages go out to those who inspired my work and motivated me to carry
on drawing throughout all the madness that goes on in the world today. This
is not a respects list (as I am soon going to give up writing those I think)
but more a warm handshake to those who I consider friends or idols (and in
same cases both), and friends who support:me and make me want to continue
this life ...                            :
                                         :
Venturus - A geniune and honestly nice surprise to see the always improving
work from yourself. Not only are you a good artist, but one of the nicest as
well. It will be a long time before I forget our chat over that bad
alchoholic drink at Assembly'97.         :
                                         :
Folar - Every release just improves in quality mate. From someone who started
relatively new to the ascii scene (I remember drawing for your first release)
your stuff has improved greatly. Always a:pleasure to leech your latest. Oh
did I mention I consider you a good friend as well? But I do ehe.
                                         :
Fatal - I never did get round to translating those swedish porno lines did I?
Well maybe next time when it's early morning and we both have nothing to do
with our lives we can get it together. Anyway it was a memorable chat even if
you did spend most of the time laughing and joking at my bad swedish accent.
Still ... I could be Norwegian hey. Knirch!
                                         :
Manta - It sounds silly but since the days of ArtCore you've always been a
huge figure in my reckoning of the scene and now for the third time I find
myself in an ascii group with you. How strange is that huh? Let's hope that
we last a little longer than Whale this time.
                                         :
U-Man - If you want a really spooky message then read the one to Manta as I
just worked out that I have the same to say to you as well! :)
                                         :
Stezotehic - Looking back at your animal releases and what absolutely quality
they are! Incredible and totally original:work my friend, you are to be
congratulated on being one of the few completely unique artists left on this
ever decaying scene. Avoid drinking coffee that might kill the green people.
                                         :
Boheme - The summer of 97 is definately one of my better memories and I think
also one of the reasons that I carry on in this game. Someone visits from a
foreign country and they are shown friendship, by someone they have only ever
chatted to on the phone. A big handshake of respect to you my friend. Your
name may never be Number One in the ascii:charts but on personality and
strength of character you're in that position in my books.
                                         ·
Despite what some people have said about me in the past. Have a drink with me
if you see me at a party. I am there to enjoy myself and meet people I only
know from collections, emails or IRC. The whole process of having a party is
to ENJOY yourself but so many people wander round saying they wish that they
had done more. Do it, don't waste your money and try to live a little.
