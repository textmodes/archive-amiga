                A colly deeply inspired by POSKGUBBE (puma/kts!a)


                Hello. I'm back in a new collection under the PUMA-

                label. Letharic has left us, so there's three of us

                left, the killing trio. Since i didn't get so many

                requests this colly is quite small, only 16 logos.

                I think i might release one more this year, IF I

                GET THOSE REQUESTS! ;). I named the colly as lame

                as possible. "The BEAST" was the lamest i could think

                of. Sounds like a "first-colly-ever", but this is

                actually my.. uhm.. 13th collection or something.

                I'd like to thank all active groups right now;

                LP!, M'S, and A! (active and active hehe), and

                all the active artists on the PC-scene and AMIGA-

                scene. KEEP IT FUCKING UP, and keep your weed burning.
