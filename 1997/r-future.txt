         I've had to do a lot of thinking about things lately. The future
       weighs heavily on my mind. What am I to do with this gift we call
       life? Where does my destiny lay? Is it in the smile of a lover,
       an embrace of a child, or somewhere down a lonely highway which
       radiates the warm dry summer heat back up towards the soles of my
       feet as I trek westward through the highways and biways of our
       civilization in search of a feeling and a place to call my own?

         I have spent my days in quiet contemplation of these wonders, and
       cannot say as though I have found any answers, only more questions
       to trouble my spirit.

         I have become sure of one solitary fact, mine is a troubled
       existence. I have been and will always be troubled by the ills one
       man may without conscience inflict upon another. The crimes of
       love, of hate, and of passion, will remain with me as long as I
       remain with you in this land of living souls. If I must be aware of
       these things, in possession of a conscience, then the least I may
       hope for is that is be social.

         The future is what we are willing to make of it, laying somewhere
       between our our brightest hopes and our darkest nightmares.

                                       - Hiro Protagonist, 04/10/97
