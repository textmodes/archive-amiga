  Hi... Yes, it's me again. As you see, I have not quit making asciis which
  some people thought. I've been very inactive lately though...
  I usually don't say very much about my collections in the prefaces because
  I think that you rather look at it than read about it. I also like to
  express my feelings in different matters that I feel is important to me,
  I believe that is more important than telling you about a collection you
  are about to watch anyway.
  However, this time everything is different. Sure, I have a lot of thoughts
  on my mind that I would like to share with you but this collection is a bit
  special so I would like to put down a few words about it.
  One thing you will notice when you start watching this collection is that
  there is not really any logos that you can use. It only consists of band,
  organisation and friend logos. I've made this collection entirely because
  these bands, organisations and persons mean a lot to me and this is my way
  to show my appreciation to them. I hope everyone can enjoy it though.
  I also would like to take the time to thank Vinzi for including me in his
  list of the best artists. That made me really proud and it was at that point
  I started to make this collection. I also "stole" the idea of including some
  personal information from him... So thank you Vinzi! You're the one who got
  me back into ascii art for real again!
  Of course I would like to thank all my friends for being there, you know who
  you are and you know I love you!
  Thank you, and until next time, goodbye!

                                                                       Niklas!
