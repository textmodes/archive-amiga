        Well here I am, in my new group Epsilon Design with my first
        release under the group label...

        I was lucky enough to find some nice Epsilon Design  members
        on  IRC  including  Fatal,  Chrombacher and Tommy, and a few
        days later it was announced to me that I had  been  accepted
        into  the  group. It's great to be with you guys!

        This one's called Glass Finger (or Fingers, as  Tommy  would
        say  ;) ...for some reason, I don't know why... And I'm sure
        you don't care.

        I've been getting a lot of requests from IRC dudes and  some
        of  them  you  will find here. To the guys who wanted logo's
        next day but didnt get them, sometimes it's  impossible  for
        me  to  do that because I'm very busy... but you should find
        your reqests in here or in my next collection.

        If you're an ascii artist, I'm looking for  logo's  all  the
        time  (Rasta, Epsilon Design, Motive) so if you have a spare
        moment please do one or two for me, thanks.

        You'll find my email address at the end of this  collection,
        use  it  to request logo's, or if you want to contact me for
        some reason.

        Also find me in IRC for requests or whatever...

                                                Rasta/Epsilon Design
