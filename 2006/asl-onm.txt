 I never liked winter, winter is a dead corpse, an empty shell waiting for yet
 another anima. Winter  is a general  anaesthetic, a painful  and failed one :
 activity stops,  living creatures get alseep; I wish I could do the same, but
 the dose is  wrong, the  dope  is not  strong  enough, I'm still  awake, in a
 partial  hibernation, just  as  pathetic as the sunlight : pale  and cold, no
                   color, no contrast : a taste of  sadness.
                :                                             :

 These last few months  within what we got used to call the scene have been as
 a long winter for me. Most of the good friends I met since I've been involved
 with it,  all those people  I loved to  work  with on a lot  of different and
 exciting projects  have been retiring  one after  another, around me is now a
                  static world where nothing happens anymore.
                :                                             :

 This collection has been  done in such an atmosphere and while it should have
 been finished in  a few weeks if I  had worked  as I used to, it took me more
 than a whole year to have everything ready. But it's there, finally, and I am
 proud to release it for the great aerosol posse, our first release this year,
           on the 21th of march, 2006, the  same day spring arrives.
