         it is 1.1.2015. at 21 i went for a run. while listening to some 2010's
         retrofuturistic 80's music,  i had an idea. for a couple of days i had
         been talking with carnifex / damones. he was the sysop of dying degree
         bbs and i was co-sysoping the system.  we talked about boards and soon
                                                        to be vintage hardware.



         while running, all these recent talks were being processed by my mind.
         and  the headphones  were pumping  this neon  colored 1980's  inspired
         electronic  music.  then i had to  admit  to  myself that  an idea had
         formed in my head. the idea was minimalism,  with hints of some pseudo
                                                              futuristic looks.



         came home. removed the running shoes and jacket. opened a beer. sat in
         front  of a screen.  and  started to  craft some character  formations
         following  the vision i had in my  head.  the outcome  is this  little
                     file with this "inverse italic" stuff made by yours truly.



         there is a chance that i unintentionally ended up ripping someone. but
         i really can't remember seeing stuff like this ever. so, less worrying
         and more intuitive keyboard bashing.  if some sharp eyed monster comes
           to say, that i've been stealing, i'll just say this colly is a fake.



         now,  the work has been started.  so far i have made 6 logos and there
         are still  several characters  that i  haven't  decided how i  will be
         drawing in  this style.  and will i have  the courage to  release this
         colly. the coming weeks will tell that.  at least at the moment i feel
                               excited about working on this new kind of stuff.

                          ok. now i know where this went. i've been typing and
                          removing things all the time.  been reminding myself
                                       that my original vision was minimalism.


                          it is a schizophrenic feeling,  when you're doing an
                          ascii colly in 2015 and  you're wanting to try a new
                          thing. and at the same time you have this thought of
                          scene worthly ascii art, that it has to fit at least
                                                      into some kind of frame.


                          at the end  i am a bit  confused did  i wuss out  at
                          some point and not go far enough. no matter what, it
                          was,  once again,  an interesting  trip to  get to a
                                             colly into a release ready state.


                          the ending  became a bit rushed,  as i'll be having a
                          vacation. couldn't leave this dangling, because there
                          is the possibility i wouldn't  find the motivation to
                                                         finish this after all.


                          year 2o14 wasn't  completely silent  for me, as i did
                          one cp437  piece for break pack #5.  i think the last
                          time before that  i tried doodling any "pc ascii" was
                          around 1994.  it was guite fun and  different kind of
                          exercise,   so  afterwards  i've   been  occasionally
                          doodling some more for my own pleasure. drawing ascii
                               is its own kind of relaxation for an adult mind.


                          it seems  like this is  going to be  the  first amiga
                          ascii release for year 2o15. apparently break managed
                          to push out pack #6 before "mnmlsm". some nice pieces
                                in there. check it out, if you haven't already.


                          see   you  in  asciiarena.com  or  ircnet  or  efnet.
