            Busting it large,its cHILL on the keys with anotha
             ascii groove to shake your bones.Its been a wild 
            few weeks for me and I've been getting involved in
             a few more projects with what I can only say are 
            the best friends that you could ask for!... big up
             the dudes that keep the faith .. they don't need 
            names .. they know who they are .. So after some  
             great chats on IRC with peeps I've come up again 
            with some ascii to chill ya soul!.I have seen some
             nice ascii lately from other artists so I'm here 
            to pay respect to all the peeps out there and hope
             you get some pleasure from this ascii experience     
                                                               
            As a form of explanation for the title and content
           of this collection.I doubt few people will recognise 
            the title as its a song from the early punk era.In
           the uK as you know Punk had a huge influence on many
            people many who are my age now.Suddenly I had the 
         notion to "recover" some old vinyl deep within the musty
           recess of my attic and listening to the old punk got
          me buzzing remembering some great times and old friends

           So this colly is a tribute to friends old and new and
          also as the Christmas season approaches some cool ascii
           gifts for friends from all over the globe.Its has not
          been the best year for me but I've got some fresh ideas
           and enjoying the asc² that the great artists that are
          on the Amiga scene are releasing.Also good to see there
           are still some active groups around as well lets hope
          it continues for many years to come.Peace to you all...
