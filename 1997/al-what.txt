| Dead at 21... It's just not some stupid TV-series, I'm talking about life.  |
| I'm 22 and when I look at the people I went to school with I feel so sad    |
| and frustrated at the same time. I feel this way because they've turned     |
| into clones of their parents. They go to work every day and some of them    |
| are even married with kids. They've changed, they are not the people I went |
| to school with. That really scares the hell out of me! "This is how our     |
| parents live, that means we have to live like this to", is that what they   |
| are thinking?! What happened to them? The happy kids I went to school with. |
| Do you really have to stop living when you turn 20? I believe not to think  |
| so. I look at myself. I ride my skateboard, I listen to weird music, I      |
| don't drink and I don't have a clue about how my life is going to look      |
| like. Basically I'm the same kid as I was back in school. I didn't fit in   |
| then and I don't fit in now.                                                |
| But there's a major point here. I'm happy! I do whatever I want to do and I |
| know who I am and where I stand. I wouldn't change that for anything in the |
| world.                                                                      |
| I didn't die at 21...                                                       |
|                                                                             |
