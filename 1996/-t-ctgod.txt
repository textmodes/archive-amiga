     This is my 40th release as a wannabe ascii artist and for me thats a
    little special so I thought i'd write a few words on the subject.  So
              I guess you have only two choices in front of you:

           · Read on and walk a little deeper into TANGo's mind
           · Skip it and move past for the rest of the crap

     Okay you're reading?  Good ...  sit back, I don't mind if you smoke,
    hell open another beer if you want as I can't tell.  Right lets begin.

    I've been drawing ascii for close on 3.5 years now and i've released a
     ridiculous number of collections.  The number of collections doesn't
     make you somebody special, it's the quality and craftsmanship within
        them that helps them to stand out.  That a piece of advice for
     everyone and something that I've finally understood.  When I was in
     Whale, I was getting enough requests to churn out a collection every
   week or so, and that's what I did.  I shouldn't have done, but it seemed
     like the right thing to do at the time.  Look at people like U-Man,
       Exocet, Mark Ryder, Mogue, H2o and all the other truly talented
      artists around on the scene.  They release maybe 6/7 normally in a
    year at a -MAXIMUM- and work a little (or maybe a lot) on their stuff
    to make it special and it works.  I just churned stuff out to keep up
                      with the requests I was getting.

    My styles have changed over the past few years, moved around and then
    almost come a full circle 40 collections later.  I've been influenced
    in my design and styles by so many people I think I could just release
     a text file with the names of those who've helped shape it all but I
      won't :) Some styles have been shit and most of the others are no
     better, but still people request from me, and for that I thank you.
    University pressure means that releases will be a little less frequent
    from me now, so please be patient and forgiving ...  I try my hardest.
     to keep everyone happy because it's in my nature, but then I wonder
    why I bother sometimes, not due to the individual, but rather the fact
    that I spent time working on a text file that will be spread (I hope)
      everywhere and maybe even used, but I could be using this time for
    other stuff, maybe a game of football, a concert, a beer instead.  The
       reason?  I have no life?  Maybe, or maybe for some dumb reason I
       actually honestly enjoy doing it and just don't understand why.
