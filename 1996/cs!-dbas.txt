Well, i`ve decided to start doing the worlds BIGGEST Artist database ever made!
All  the  previous  released  mags for the ART business try to be UP TO DATE in
released collections and votetopics ...  nargh!  ...  Actually they`re not!

So  i  got  the idea to make a kinda chain-letter to ALL the artists around and
all  the  artists  BEEING  around  the  last years!  GEM and all the others who
quitted the scene (SKIN, AMBLIN etc.) are INCLUDED!

The  main  idea is to get all the ARTISTS, their affiliated groups (past>today)
and  all their collections in LENGTH, RELEASE DATE and under which group it has
been released ..  all in all It should like this

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Artist           Groups                 Collections           Length       Date
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FLI7e            Caustic Style          Numero Uno            12.345   10/10/92
                                        Numero Zwei          145.678   20/12/93
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Database will have Sort functions for example: Longest Collection(s)
                                                   Most Active Artist
                                                   Most used Groups

and some others if possible!

The DBASE Will have GUI (or mui) ^ ascii/ansi viewing ^ Print function and many
more (not that lame as in the xCz mag ..  sorry pal!)

So  if  you  are  interested in cooperating with me and the whole CS!  posse by
doing  this  DBASE  ...   Well,  we`ve  the  ASM coder with about 7 years Amiga
Demoscene  coding  an  various releases ...  WIGWAM / SAD ...  We also have the
SATAN`S  FIRE/Roadhogz^Caustic  Style  who collected many many details over the
months!

BUT:  We need YOU to get the REAL facts `bout YOUR person!  Because who else is
able to tell them most truthly?!

Also  if  you  send us the information ...  Include a small description of your
own  (about  a  maximum textlenth of 5k) and we`ll put it straight to the dbase
entry of your name!

Letus make the ART SCENE more popular and more Informative than it is nowadays!

Letus  also  proof that the AMIGA ascii business is the best of them all!  Fuck
the  PC artists who can make good looking COLOR ARTS but are most over NOT able
to  make some with slashes and add-on chars like the most artists (me, too) use
to realize their asciis!

All  in  all  it`s  a  mega project which will for sure take me a LONG while to
realize  but  i`ll let you know (the artscene) about the growing and I`ll (if i
get  some  replys) release a list of artists which are included once or twice a
month.
