   How about you, dear reader?  How  looked your first
   pictures, dear artist?  And your first asciis, dear
   maker? How sounded your first music, dear composer?
   And what about your first effects, dear coder? Hmh?
   Everything as everybody  has  to  start  somewhere,
   sometime... So maybe you remember how lame - if you
   are no god - the things were you  produced  at  the
   beginning  of  your  career.  So I wonder why these
   days are so many `elite` guys around behaving  like
   gods  by  treating beginners in a very bad way, not
   remembering about their beginning in the scene. Why
   do  they  forget  about friendship, the base of OUR
   scene? Why do they forget about  OUR  future,  that
   has to be continued by someone who is living longer
   than us? It seems equal how needed they are & apart
   from their importance for the  present  and  future
   scene, necomers knock on wood.
   I think you better catch your own nose (as a german
   slogan says)  and  give  a chance to newcommers and
   beginners.  Help  them to get better and don`t stay
   always on your own, same limits!!! Don`t be boring,
   be innovative!

                                    Zito on o5.o8.2ooo
