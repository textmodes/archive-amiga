  Infinity is hard to comprehend.
  I couldn't hear those screams even
  in my wildest dreams.

  Scared to fall asleep again incase
  the dream begins again. Someone
  chasing, I cannot move. Standing
  rigid nightmare's statue. What a
  dream, when will it end and will
  i transcend?

  Restless sleep the minds in turmoil.
  One nightmare end another fertile.
  Getting to me so scared to sleep,
  but scared to wake now, in too deep.

  Even though its reached new heights.
  I rather like restless nights. It
  makes me wonder it makes me think
  there's more to this I'm on the brink.
  It's not the fear of what's beyond.
  It's just that i might not respond.
  I have an interest almost craving, but
  would I like to get too far in?
