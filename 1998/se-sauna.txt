                               Another collection. Another year.
                               My heart still burns compassion towards
                               ASCII. Lots of shit has happened since
                               I was active the last time.. I've got work
                               up to my neck ALL THE TIME so I haven't
                               had the time to keep up with the ascii
                               releases of today. Who's the biggest now?
                               Any releases at all? When one does computing
                               for living, it dies as a hobby.

                               It's funny how so many ascii artists
                               do Web design! Too many to mention, heh..
                               I guess the designing principles in both
                               ascii and Web are quite the same. Stretching
                               the techniques to the extreme to create
                               miracles. Even as the Web is seen as
                               "HI-TECH", I only see it as primitive shit
                               difficult to handle. Maybe when every flat
                               has a 128MB line it could really change our
                               daily life.

                               At the moment the Net is in such a ridiculous
                               state - We have the equipment, but what's the
                               target with it? What's the use? What we need
                               is good services to provide the end users
                               with. It'd be a pity to see Net only become
                               a merchandising forum.
