Oh  well  another intro have to be written by me again doh!  im starting to
get SOOOO tired of this. Its the second one today sigh!

So to inform you,and also to answer Tango's question.

Why  are  you  doing  this co-op with the hero from Midbank which doesnt do
anything at work,besides making ascii's ?  Well in the infamous series Dope
On Plastic,we had these small msgs going back and forth in our collys about
this and that.So i started to like this guy,and i feelt like there was this
special  bondage  between  us,and i think Tangos got this special charme in
his  collys,and  i  really love his work,and i wanted to combine my smoking
artwork(?)  with  his  charme  ;)  and since both of us release quite a few
collys  a  month,i thought that we had enough motivation for doing this one
between our orther projects,if any.

As  you might notice in my part of this sucker,my style(s) isnt the same as
you've  been used to see from me,this is because most of the logos are very
old,  eg.  6-8 months.They where meant to be in Flush Part 3,but due to the
extreme  lazynes  of  the  coder,yes i said coder for those who havent seen
flush part 2,which is build up like a reguler diskmag almost,but instead of
articles  then youll watch some ascii work,BUT,as i started on,the coder is
so  fucking  lazy  that  all  logos  is very old now,and instead of waiting
another  couple  of years for the coder to finnish the code,ill release the
colly  now  but  under  another  name,like the gfx for flush 3 is drawn and
all,so  why  waste  the  gfx  when  you just draw a new asci logo for a new
name.So  some  of  the logos is very ugly,some arent but thats life..but if
you  cant  stand to watch my logos in this colly,then ill release a new one
in a very short time,with my normal/new style so watch out for that okay?

There  have  been a couple of txt files around saying that this compo which
devistator tryes to make,that hes a 'wannabe' just as artmag are it said...
and  they  cant come into our ascii scene without any knowlege or inside...
so  to  those who says that why dont you just try to make a compo/chart etc
instead of complaining??

Enough words from me...read Tangos stroy......

Before I start ... a few little words about this collection ...

I  looked  at work by Desoto, Bastard, Xcluziwe, Mogue, Chrombacher, Fatal &
U-Man  and  thought whats the point in releasing a collection with any style
similar  to theirs as I'd be outclassed, when people request a logo from me,
they request it because they like my style, not because they want me to draw
a  desoto  dot rip-off logo, so I'm just sticking with my usual style (yawn)
because I say:  "go all the way ...  or go home!" ...  so I went home :)

If  anyone  accuses  me  of ripping ...  I tell them they're a liar because
no-one else has a style quite as boring as mine!  Hur hur hur ...

Here is the multiple choice recommendations for reading my ascii:

1) Use the POT-NOODLE font because everything looks a LOT better ... OR
2) Close yar eyes ... it looks incredible!

After  much consideration I've come to the conclusion that there's no point
talking about yar girlie in a collection ...  unless she trades (or sits on
ya  shoulder  while  you read the collections hehe) she will NEVER see your
little  dedication  to  her  pretty face.  Aaah fuck it, we've got to prove
we've  got  lives outside out computers somehow haven't we?  Well, everyone
but me I suppose :)

Relief?   Never  heard  of him ...  Hehe ...  collection drawn 'cause he's a
hero  (yup it's time to stroke his ego haha) and a lot better than me, so if
I've  got  his  artwork  in  here  it's  bound  to improve the quality of my
collection isn't it?

Xcz mentioned in his last collection about the ascii scene ...  he's right,
it's  become  a massive industry over the last year becoming a scene in its
own  right,  but  it's  not  the group, the person's name, or the boards he
calls that make him a good artist ...  it's the inspiration from within ...
his real artistic personality ...
