                           «« aBOUT eJECT! »»

This collection was done over 3 days in March 1994. It contains 3O ASCII
descriptions. If you like any of them, then use them for your uploads or
whatever. But please don't alter them in any way, or remove the "EL?".
As well as the usual desriptions for cracking groups, I've also included a
few demo groups for good measure!

The collection is named after an E.P. by the band "Senser", just in case
you're interested!

But before I get on with the collection, I think I'll just do a quick plug
for Divine's forthcoming UKHQ. It should be open in only a few weeks from
now, and the number will be spread closer to the time. Please support this
board, we will reward your support (fnarr!)
