                          -+- first words: -+-






        to  start  with, i would like to give a word to all of you
        who  think  i  am  a "ripper" etc.  i have many times been
        claimed  of  ripping loggos from deSoto/e^d.  i guess it's
        mostly  because  of  my "dot" letters.  let me give you my
        side  of  the  story:  i think deSoto is a very good ascii
        maker.  and i think that "dots" in ascii logos is almost a
        "must",  yes,  to  me it is.  and because of this my ascii
        may  look "a bit" like desotos.  but i don't see the point
        really.   i  could  line  up  hundreds (?) of ascii makers
        using "dots" in their logos (i bet deSoto wasn't the first
        one...).    and   why  do  i  use  theese  "dot"  letters?
        simple...  it's because i think they look good.  maybe you
        could  compare  it  with an musician who likes jazz.  what
        music  does  a  musician  that  likes  jazz?   that's also
        simple...   he makes jazz music...  or...  ehh...  well...
        i think you get it.

        you  could  also see it from another point of view.  i may
        be  one  of  thoose  "cover  artists".   i  make covers of
        desotos  logos.  simply ripping them off and changing some
        stuffs.  NAAAAAAAAAAHHHH!!!  you see...  thats not the way
        it's  done.  ever heard of something called "inspiration"?
        it's  something VERY good.  many artists and musicians use
        "inspiration"  in  their works.  yes, i think almost every
        artist  of  every  kind  need  some  kind  of inspiration.
        maybe.  (i'm getting to the point soon).  i am also one of
        thoose strange guys that needs "inspiration" in my work.

        well...   let's  skip  all  this shit now (hello?  are you
        still  there?).   urm...   anyway.   i  think  it's kind'a
        boring  making  these  ascii logos.  and there are so many
        ascii  artists  in  sweden,  and it's very hard keeping up
        with  the  best,  and,  and,  and...   sure...  and if you
        happens  to  get  an request one day, you can be sure that
        866  other  ascii  makers  in sweden got the same request.
        what  is  the  problem?   do you really need 866 different
        logos?   or  is  it  some  nice hobby you've got?  i mean,
        fooling  around all thoose ascii artists with requests you
        dont  really  need...   thats  all  with the first words i
        think.   i  don't feel like writing any more now.  i guess
        everything  i've  written  so far is pure bullshit.  but i
        don't  care.   at least i think i've said all i wanted to.
        and  who  knows?   this might be the last ascii collection
        made  by me...  well...  if i got some serios requests and
        so,  i  might consider making a fourth ascii collection...
        only time will tell...
