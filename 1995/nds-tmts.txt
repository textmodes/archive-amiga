                ===>- Last interview from [c-REAL!] ? -<===



Q: [c-REAL!], the lastest interview we did was when you
   where to release your colly 'Million dollar mouth'. What
   has happened sine then?

A: Well, quite a lot actually. This is the third since p^d-mdm!.txt
   and I think that my style have changed quite a lot since then.
   I've joined Nine Design, a couple of guys building up something
   that hopefully will be good.

Q: Many artists have expressed a sort of relief when you abadoned
   the c-style featured in f.e Surviving in scene and Keeping my word.

A: That's right. The c-style as you mentioned was quite easy to
   draw, and I thought it looked quite good. But one must continue
   and explore new sides of the keyboard. To me 'Million dollar..'
   was the official farewell to the c-style although I tried some
   other styles in 'And the story continues..'

Q: In this 15th collection of yours you present a new style?

A: Yes, I've been trying do develope a alternative to the style
   you find in 'The Sign' and even in this colly. This new
   c-treetstyle is quite effectful and I think it looks great
   as a alternative.

Q: Aren't you afraid of that somebody else writes in the same way?

A: People borrow things everyware. It's my intention to write in
   a new style, but as I've written in the Art-collection if anyone
   gets offended thinking I've ripped their style, please mail me.

Q: You said 'Art-collection'?

A: Yes, it's ascii that I write, but I think that ascii is a
   art when made by great artists.

Q: Is there any special artist around that you admire?

A: Admire is a very strong word, but I must say that I'm very
   impressed of Fatal&Chrombacher's 'Plan 9 from outer space'.

Q: Rumors that you are quitting the asciiscene are true?

A: I've heard that rumor myself, but there are no facts behind that.
   I going to continue as usual.

Q: That means with on e collection a week?

A: Hehe, no. Last time I said I would release them with a month between,
   but since I've had many requests I had to release a collection.
   But I've done so much lately so I'm going to take some time off,
   spending more time on other important hobbies. Feeding ducks in
   the park, work as a DJ, rubber-sex,

Q: Rubber-sex??

A: Well, maybe not, but I have other things to take care of.

Q: But this is neither the last collection nor the last interview
   from you?

A: Noway, I'm back before you know it.

Q: Well, do you have anything else to tell the world before signing
   off?

A: I would like all you Art-writers around the globe to include
   a 'c-real' and a 'Pioneer Design'-logo in their next collections.

Q: No political statements?

A: You find them in the collection..

Q: Okey [c-REAL!], nice talking to you again. Take care until
   next time!

A: I will, thank you!
