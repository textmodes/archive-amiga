 GRAVES TO GO ON A NEW MISSION! A MISSION FROM THE -LORD- HIMSELF!

WRITTEN ON 1ST MAY 1995...
2FAST... QUALITY SEPERATES THE MAN FROM THE BOYS!... AFTER I JOINED THE
 GERMAN MILITARY LAST MONTH! I WASN'T HAPPY! BEING AWAY FROM HOME, BEING AWAY
 FROM MY GIRLFRIEND AND MY BOOZE-PARTNERS AND SOME MORE...! BUT I SURVIVED
 THE FIRST FOUR WEEKS AT THE FELDJAEGER(MP)...! MUCH FUN SHOOTING WITH THE NEW
 G36!... FEELING A LITTLE BIT LIKE RAMBO! HAHA... NOPE! WE HAVE TO PROTECT
 OUR COUNTRY AND NOT TO START A NEW WORLD WAR...! AS AFTER 50 YEARS OF GLORY
 FREEDOM ALL COULD BE POSSIBLE! SO I HOPE IT WILL NEVER HAPPEN AGAIN! I AM
 STILL IN CONTACT WITH SOME OF MY SCENE-FRIENDS, BUT I SOLD MY OLD MODEM
 TO A SCENE-LAMER, WHO PAYES ME 400 GERMAN BUGS FOR MY OLD USR 14.4K ONLY!
 HAHA... FUCK HIM!

WRITTEN ON 7TH MAY 1995...
WELL, TODAY I JUST BOUGHT A NEW MODEM FROM AN OLD SCENE-MATE, WHO ALREADY
 QUITTED THE SCENE IN JANUARY THIS YEAR, FOR A GOOD PRIZE... IT'S JUST A
 ZYXEL, BUT BETTER THEN A USR 14.4HST ONLY! STAY ON THE EDGE, I AM BACK

BUT NOW ENJOY THE SHOW...                    2FAST/[ARTCORE/HOODLUM/NIGHTFALL]
