  |\/_/ The days are getting darker and the winter is coming in hard and     |
  |     fast up here in the far north. Outside another yellow moon has       |
  | ::  punched a whole in the night time. I'm cuddled up in front of the    |
  | ::  fireplace, letting the flickering light of the fire cheerfully       |
  |     play on my wild hair and beard and cast shadows on the walls. In     |
  |     my hands I'm holding the ancient drink of chimarrao, from which      |
  | ::  I occasionally sip a mouthful of the ragged and ecstatic joy of      |
  |     pure being.                                                          |
  |                                                                          |
  |     To be a wee bit honest with you.. This autumn hasn't been all        |
  |     good though but quite a challenge for me and as often times          |
  |     before I've searched refugee in the nature. I've been reading one    |
  |     too many Jack London novels and spent much time on the roads         |
  |     across the country visiting national parks and other spots of        |
  |     wild nature. Daydreaming of the simple life that is just out of      |
  |     of reach and experiencing that humbling almost religious feeling     |
  |     that you can find in the solitude and beauty of old-growth forest.   |
  |     The nature therefore is the theme for my first Divine Stylers        |
  |     ascii art collection and I feel priveleged to represent my old       |
  |     heroes. From the start I wanted this colly to mean something         |
  |     and to be personal, atleast in a fragmented kind of way. It's        |
  |     my 'West side story' or my 'Born to run', if you like. But you       |
  |     have to figure out what it means by yourself.                        |
  |                                                                          |
  |     Special thank-you-very-much's goes to a fellow gothenburgensis-      |
  |     Skope, for drawing the Divine stylers header which I used in the     |
  |     colly and to my dear old comrade in the scene, Pogue, who drew       |
  |     the impure!ascii logo. I'd like to also pass out a cheerio to     :: |
  |     Griskokare for helping out with that joint piece and for being a     |
  |     good friend. Same thing goes to Caphood, Mr. Fiddle, the             |
  |     undisputed pirate king of the BBS world. Great guy.               :: |
  |                                                                       :: |
  |     .. There's something larger than all this behind the horizon and  __ |
  |     over the mountains, so gather up around the fire and I'll tell   /\_\|

   "I believe that life is a mess," he answered promptly. "It is like yeast,
   a ferment, a thing that moves and may move for a minute, an hour, a year,
    or a hundred years, but that in the end will cease to move. The big eat
    the little that they may continue to move, the strong eat the weak that
      they may retain their strength. The lucky eat the most and move the
          longest, that is all. What do you make of those things?"

men det är vägen som är mödan värd.

där elden tänds och bryts i hast.

blir sömnen trygg och drömmen full av sång.

Oändligt är vårt stora äventyr.
