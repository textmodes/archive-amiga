      i really didn't expect to make another colly. during the last three
      years, i was just drawing some  logos from time to time... but then
      asciiarena  came  along!..   spot & dmg deserve a big fucking scene
      award.  and tons of hugs.  asciiarena.com  is an ingenious project, 
      and  neither  this  colly  nor  many  others  would  have  surfaced 
      without  this effort.  finally  an  ascii site which is not an ugly 
      forum  but  a  full  blown  portal  project. 

      the  logos  you will see in this colly are drawn between  2007  and
      2010,  and  represent  small but constant variations in  my  style.
      both  reqs  and gifts are ibcluded. in  some  cases  i  forgot  who
      requested the stuff, because it was so long ago.

      the  colly  is  obviously named after the great spanish  civil  war
      epos  by  ken  loach,  showing people getting their act together to
      build a better life against all odds. so, i  guess  i  should  cram
      a  few  durruti quotes in here - but i won't, instead i  will  make
      some  adverts  for  noteworthy  and  support-worthy scene projects. 

      well,  not  much to  say  anymore, except  for  that  i  am back on 
      track!  let's  see  how  much  time  a  doctorate allows to release
      a  colly  once  in  a  few  months.

      a  special  shoutout to my aerosol mates - let's kick it  in  2010!

      i  am  taking  reqs   again,   please   feel   free   to   mail  me 
      at      dipswitch  (at)  kaoz  (dot)  org  if   you   need   logos.

      - dipswitch/aerosol, january 2010.
