                   asciiscene.org Ascii competition - Week 46
                   ------------------------------------------

            The first asciiscene.org ascii competition is at its end.
            The number of contributers this time were: 15
            and the number of voters were: 18.

            The theme for the first compo was to draw a
            'asciiscene.org' logo.



                       These were the rules to be obeyed:
                       ----------------------------------

    o1. amiga style ascii only (no ansi, no tools - pure skillz)
        - all other works will be disqualified
    o2. size max 78x25
    o3. ascii should be "readable" with topaz and p0t-n00dle
    o4. post ascii in the thread responding to this week's compo
    o5. don't post crap in this forum - stuff not relating to this compo
    o6. deadline for artists - one week from the start of the compo thread
    o7. after deadline, voters will have three days to get and vote on the
        logos in a poll
    o8. only one contribution per artist
    o9. a random guy from the ascii scene is selected to choose a theme for
        the colly each week
    1o. the very same random guy also assembles the final ascii




                               Voting rules were:
                               ------------------

              Cast your vote on your three (3) favourite ascii´s.

                your no.1 ascii gets 5 points
                your no.2 ascii gets 3 points
                your no 3 ascii gets 1 point

                - The syntax shall look like:
                <nick1> 5 points
                <nick2> 3 points
                <nick3> 1 points

                - You are not allowed to vote on your own ascii!
                - You are only allowed to vote once!
                - Deadline for votes is 3 days.
