        || Is    the    ascii-scene   dead?    Well,   at   least   the ||
        || ascii-request-scene  is  dead.   There  seems  no one really ||
        || interested  in  this  kind  of art anymore.  From all over I ||
        || hear  ascii-artists  crying:   "Please request something!!!! ||
        || Even  a  single logo!  Please..." What`s up?  Everywhere you ||
        || await  an  ascii,  there  is  one,  but  only olders, used a ||
        || thousand  times.   Only  stuff  more than two years overdue. ||
        || Even  some  groups use file_id.diz they have since they are. ||
        || The  logos  are  always  and everywhere the same.  No wonder ||
        || that  todays  ascii-artists  are  lazy  and once legendaries ||
        || don`t care about doing somthing new for the scene.           ||
        ||                                                              ||
        || You  may  say  I am not THAT big in ascii-bizz, that`s true, ||
        || but  I  am  in  contact  with legends besides trying to make ||
        || ascii  by myself as good as it gets and I know the problems. ||
        || Even  if you offer someone to make him some design or logos, ||
        || he  will say:  "Hmh, I like the old one?  Why change it?" or ||
        || "Of  course, next time!" In my eyes it hurts to see the same ||
        || logos  from several BBS since years.  If the scene continues ||
        || treating  ascii-makers  this  way we don`t have to wonder if ||
        || the ascii-scene really dies one day!                         ||
