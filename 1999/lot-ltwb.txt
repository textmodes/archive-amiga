===============[ In The First Moment Of The Beginning We Start To Die... ]===



 Little Introduction

 Without a reason I had the idea to visit a czech party I read about on a
 nice private amigasite I found somewhere while I was sitting in my bureau
 without some interesting work going through the !nternet instead of doing
 things my boss wanted me to do...
 The week-end after I called Angeldust and we had a long and cool
 `Plauderstunde` where we decided to travel together to the party and
 release a picture by Angeldust, a chiptune by me and maybe an intro by
 Apathy...
 Than I made a phonecall to Punisher and he was surprised and told me to
 send a disk with a new 4k-intro by Zeeball. Well, it seemed that after I
 was walking in the scene some years I would come to an foreign party
 although I am not that kind of party freak who is boozing all the time...
 So I send a little e-mail to Lahve and called him the same evening to make
 all things (food, place etc.) clear and he was happy because he got only
 four reservations until this day, monday five days before the start...
 But what seemed so bright turned out into darkness! Angeldust called me on
 thursday to tell that the german train company has to high prices for the
 way from Hamburg to Leipzig. In a sad mood I prepared everything to go
 on saturday alone, but maybe because of some function problems deep in my
 sick brain I slept until 13 o`clock although I had to stand up at 6 to
 arrive 10 o`clock in czech party place. If I had stand up the right time
 I would have visited but I didn`t really want to come alone and at last
 not too late. So I spend the week-end in front of my tv-set and watched
 some cool videotapes...
