I bought the US Robotics HST Courier Dual Standard 14k4 modem.

Liberator. I didn't believe my eyes!, every new game released was on the board!

he quit the board, i took over his userlist and started my own board
called "The altar of sacrifice".

When i needed some logo's for my board I started to make some ascii's by myself,
damned those were ugly!!! but I tried, and I tried, and I tried, till someone 
asked me to make some for them too, and within no time I got more requests.

The board excisted for about 5 years and I loved watching users login, but when
the Amiga "died", the Altar died with it. But it never died for me!!!, I still
have my old amiga and since january 2005 I put The Altar back online, only not
with 0-1 day warez :))) but just for fun.

About a year ago I found out that boondocks still excisted!, this was the
beginning of a new start, I found some of my old ascii collections back and
found out that the ascii scene was still alive. 

This inspired me so much to become an ascii artist again.

So here I am (again)....
