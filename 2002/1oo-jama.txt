    We will make it short, just some words and an index...

    The idea for this colly came some months ago from Browallia/Nukleus,
    a  good  friend  of  Zito  and  organiser of the swedish Jamaica ROM
    party. Due to several 1oo% members moving to another  apartment  and
    Zito getting into serious troubles (first a  major  harddisk  crash,
    then  his computer burned out) it was delayed again and could not be
    released at the named party itself.  But though it will be a Jamaica
    ROM release  as  Browallia wanted to include our ascii-collection in
    the  official  party release package! This early release - you maybe
    noticed that the party pack is still not available - is just  caused
    by some big hardware problems of him.

    So, here we are - Miracle, Cru & Zito on behalf of the 1oo% clique -
    showing you that oldskool still rules and will never die!

    The  title  of  the  colly  should be understandable (because of the
    party where it should have been released at first), but  the  design
    around  might  cause  questions. We even do not know exactly what it
    means  at  the  end,  but  we  can  tell  you  that  Zito  saw  some
    documentation  movies  about  the events at the bikini atoll (one of
    those big mistakes the USA did in their row of  doing  things  wrong
    - will  they  ever learn?) and this way he might have asked himself,
    how far mankind will go. And with his  too  science-fiction  mind  a
    story started in his head while designing this new ascii-colly...
