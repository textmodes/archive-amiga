  1. Why some more charts ?
     ----------------------
     Because there was no possibility to vote fast for all
     other mags via E-Mail or Internet ...
     So we give you the possibility to vote via Email or on our Homepage.

  2. Who should vote ?
     -----------------
     All haters, lovers, makers, sysops and users of the famous ascii art

  3. How to vote ?
     -------------
     Three ways to vote for our mag ...

     the 1st : Just surf to "http://members.aol.com/ansiattack"
               and click on the VOTE button, enter your votings
               in the online-voting-form and submit them directly
               to the email of the ansi-attack team.

     the 2nd : cut out the voting form from this textfile,
               fill out with your votings, send it via email
               to : "ansiattack@aol.com"

     the 3rd : if you're a user of the straight hate bbs just type
               vote at the mainpromt and fill out the votesheet

     the first issue will be released at 01.06.1997,
     as viewable html file on the ascii attack homepage and
     as textfile spreaded on all wellknown boards.
     If you want get the AsciiAttack Textfile via Email
     send the Ascii-Attack team your Email addy with the
     information that you want be on the mailing list for
     the ascii attack online charts.
