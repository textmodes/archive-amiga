((___( introduction )_________________________________________________________))


       welcome  to  hell,  sinner  -  here  is your copy of windows. no,
       that's  a  different  story...  welcome  to another collection of
       ascii  werx  by  neurodancer,  released  under the classic weirdo
       label  of 1oo%. my last collection "goa mantra" was, as you might
       know,  released  in  arid  weed. at that time I thought arid weed
       still  could  be  great fun - but it all got very destructurized,
       and  even  if it was mainly for the fun of it,  I missed a little
       bit of a concept. but that is past and gone, and with dipswitch's
       "rebirth"  you already saw that we're now indeed keeping the 1oo%
       ascii  spirit  alive,  and  1oo% is, despite being a bunch of the
       most  lazy guys around, the group I'm still in with all my heart.

       many  of  us  have gone different ways, yet we're still connected
       somehow,  and thats more scene and friendship spirit than you can
       ever plan to have. 1oo% is something strange that developped over
       the years, and I doubt it will ever really break apart as long as
       we're able to touch a keyboard now and then. but enough steenkin'
       self praising...  on to the part that  belongs to the collection.

       most  of  the  art  (is it?) in this rambling textfile is not the
       usual  stuff like requests and work that I could use myself. most
       of  these  logos  are made after random thoughts and phrases that
       came  into my mind during the past few pretty dark weeks of life.

       not  only that I came home from a wonderful holiday in the warmth
       and  light,  at  the beaches and in the cities of tunisia, I also
       had  to  claim  the  possible  loss  of one of the most wonderful
       persons  I've  ever  met  in my life. but, this time, the luck of
       love  seems  to  be  playing at my side, and with one desperately
       awaited  sign  of life from that person and a long long telephone
       call,  my  mood  turned  around for the better, and the things to
       come fill me with joy, and luck, and the things we're now able to
       await  are  the  best  prospect  into the future I had for a long
       time.  I doubt you can understand that, but I felt this had to be
       said - once more.

       on with the show.
