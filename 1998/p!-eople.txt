It comes like this: muted cries, the sound of panting, a crimson wave  lapping
at  his  feet. Unblinking eyes that will not turn away, the soft pressure of a
mouth at his groin; the crimson waves crashing higher and higher still,  until
he  can  taste  it  in  his  mouth, bile  and copper, blood streaming from the 
corners of his eyes, and the voice he hears is his own, the  screams  are  his
own though the blood, always, is not.

It comes like this, that you are too busy with other things, that  you  cannot
do the logos for your next ascii release. It comes like this, that you are, if
you  finally  found  some time to do so, that you're bored of the ascii scene,
and therefore you postpone your next release again.

It comes like this, it came like this: I started this collection some 8 months
ago, and delayed it for various reasons. But! here  I  am,  yet  with  another
presentation of useless ascii characters for your pleasure.

As you may see it consists of only *six* logos, five of which are  requests  I
during  the last two days on irc @ #ascii. Recently I was quite often asked if
Protest is dead. Well, what can I see? Last time I heard  a  word  from  Data,
with  whom  I  founded Protest back  in  summer'95  on a scene meeting held by
Birdhouse Projects in the "Rheinauen" in Bonn  (which  was  also  attended  by
Mogue and H2o! who had just hours before founded C!ontra ;]), is more than one
year  ago.  The  last time I released a collection under the Protest label was
around summer/fall 1997, Data hasn't released one for more than  two  years  I
think.  Protest  hasn't  been  dead until now, but silent. I'm going tired and
bored of Protest and would be happy if I could join up with one of  the  major
ascii groups in the near future. In fact, I already had some  nice  dcc  chats
with  the  members  of  one  special group, but who knows what the next day'll
confronts you  with?  So  this  may  be  the  last  collection of Protest, but
definetly the first in 1998 and last but not least presumably not the last  of
me, graveDancer (@poboxes.com) of Protest, Desaster and Neutron ;]

It comes  like  this, he comes like this: and afterward is left gasping on the
shore, the tide receding, and nothing left but that taste in his mouth, and  a
trace of blood on his palms.
