   Well, at the end I was not able to finish the texts at sunday morning
         until 6 o'clock. Too tired, too drunken by the music and too many
 cigarettes. Simple not able to order my thoughts and write them down. Once
   again another colly was planed to be something special but at the
                           end it is a try only...

   For me the science dealing with embryos is worse than slavery. Human
    life treated like commodities, like dead things. Isn't it enough
              that we treat pigs, sheeps and cows this way???
    And what makes a two months old baby less worth to live compared to
       a three months old one? This way we could start to devide
     between black and white again, something we should definitively not!
  World politics and human behavor make me doubt, make me struggle.
   Once I thought I could change the world, a revolution for a better way
      to live. That again who can? I knew I was not able to, not allowed,
                                         restricted to my own failures.

   But here lies the chance! I can change everything. Yes, as everything
        starts in small. It is not the whole world concerning.
           It is not the thing to change the planet earth on your own,
      but to begin by yourself... This is a world, my world, which only
                       I can change, where I make the laws.
   I am not able and not allowed to make others think what I think - and
     this concerns the human rights too, which are "only" displaying
           our western world's mentality. I can only change my own
      world and this way I can show others my ideas of living.
              But this is more I can change then most others ever
           changed in their whole life!
 And there - in my world - it does not matter what happens outside. This
  shall not be a pleading for self-sufficiency or allow somebody to forget
         about the earth as planet, because everybody of us is at
           least physically connected to it.
   But In my own world I can find my freedom and power to live.
                     This does also not mean retreat or lazyness,
               because as mentioned exactly there - in my own world -
        starts the changing. And nothing's gonna change my world than me.
