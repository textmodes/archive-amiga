     Greetings... Well it has been about 1 year since I have been back in
  the Amiga scene and I haven't released anything in about a 1 1/2 years.
  I am going to skip the greet list for now, since I see alot of the people
  I have known have either left the scene, got busted, or dropped off the
  face of the earth...I will probably regain contact with most of you.

     The main purpose of this release is for /X boards with PC subs. /X
  doesn't support FILE_ID.DIZ, and most PC group logos use the PC font which
  look terrible with the Amiga's TOPAZ font. I didn't use any ALTERNATE
  characters, so these can also be used on PC BBS's. They should be readable
  from a PC also. Be sure that the lines don't excede the limit (44/8)

  Anyway, lets get To the REAL(UN) stuff...I think you will like these!

                              WHAT IS UnReaL?
                              ---------------

         UnReaL is a group of people dedicated to ANSI/ASCII art.

 be more popular than it already is (eg. Charts, DiskMags, Competitions).

    We strongly urge all of you ANSI psychoz out there to start forming
                 ANSI Groups, Mags, Charts, Games(?), etc.
