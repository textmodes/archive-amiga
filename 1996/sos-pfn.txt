Introduction:
-=-=-=-=-=-=-
Thats right!  Tesko is proud to present a decent UK party at last!
The party was due to be held in October this year, however, Silk/Ex Dtl
told me he had every intention of holding a party this year so I slowed
down my plans AND cancelled the provisional date I had booked with the venue.

The very same day, I checked my email and Silk told me he had scrapped his
party plans!!!! "Arse!" - I thought!


Where Is The Party?
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-
The Party is to be held in Morecambe, in the North West of England.
The Venue itself is called "The Superdome", which is a large Dome shaped
building on the Promenade.

Morecambe is close to Lancaster, Preston and Manchester.

Morecambe is also a seaside resort, which means that theres the following:

 * Arcades
 * Bowling Alley
 * Drive Thru Burger King
 * Shops
   And much more, all close by!

"The Morecambe Tourist Board" has kindly agreed to sponser the event by
paying for the venue, so for all you long travellers, why not stop the
night in Morecambe.  Theres lots of Hotels and Bed+Breakfasts open and
prices start around 10 UKPounds per night.  Please consider staying over,
as this event would not have been possible without the Tourist Board's
help.

For more information on Hotels/B+B's, telephone Morecambe Tourism on: 
+44 (0)1524 582808 and remember to tell them that your coming to the
"Computer Conference!" :)

Voting:
-=-=-=-

When you arrive at the party, you will be given a voting sheet, which you
will be to keep for later on.  When the competition entries have been
played/shown, you will need to fill in the relevant sections with your
votes.  The votes will be counted by Tesko, with an independant adjudicator
picked at random from the crowd to ensure "fair play".

The winner of The Baked Bean and The Extra Strong Mint compo's will be
the first person to complete the task.  The items MUST be completely
eaten, and the entrants mouth EMPTY!

The Winner of the karaeoki will be voted for by the crowd, as will the 
DJ.  This can be awarded for best set, or maybe for DJ skill, it is upto
the crowd to decide.

As stated above, the Venue has been kindly paid for by Morecambe Tourism.
This means that Tesko does not have to use ticket/door money to pay for

ALL TICKET/DOOR MONEY GOES BACK INTO PRIZES!!!!!!  yeehaa!

all members of Tesko have agreed to put 20 pounds of their own cash in
to start the kitty off.

of the ticket/door money, so the more people that turn up, the more
the prizes will be.

prizes and dictate what you shall win!  Who wants to win a piece of hardware
that they have already or is not as good as their own?
