                                    PREFACE!
                                   ----------
   Respect! I guess that single word sums up this collection quite perfectly.
   It features logos for my favourite groups and artists in the ascii scene.

   I started working on this collection before I released Pa Kua, but since I
   came up with a completely new style I decided to start all over again from
   scratch. So now my style needs all kind of feedback, good as well as bad.

                                             - TOMMY/ALTERN^LOOKERHOUSE^NOICE
