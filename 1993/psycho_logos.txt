                                  - 1 -

                   Opening Words About "Psycho Logos #1"

    Well, here we are with my very FIRST ansi/ascii release.. I never
    really intended on doing such a collection, but we all know how
 
    that goes.. Ansi/Ascii is becoming very popular these days and its
    great to have a variety of work to choose from and come up with new
    ideas.. In any case, enough of the red tape bullshit and here's my
    addition to the ansi/ascii archives.. Enjoy!
