   I  was  sitting  in  my room,  and  i  got this idea Printed out all
   my  asciis! Rolled a  big  joint.  Lighted  it up!  Inhale,  exhale!

   At   first  i  thought  it   wouldn't   work,   but  suddenly i  had
   this good feeling, i was seeing  things!  wonderfull shapes of ascii
   was afront  of me, and i was dancing  among them. They were  kissing
   me. They  were  my  lovers, then i  smoked more, and i  saw  several
   asciis   taking  form.  This  is  how  it   all   started   when   i
   was  sitting  in my room seeing these wonderfull  shapes, the spirit
   of the ascii touched my inner soul, and the results you now can see!
