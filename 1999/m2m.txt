            Viewing instructions
            --------------------

            1. The editor you use have to be able to show more than
               1000 lines, or you won't see the entire colly.

            2. You have to use A) an Amiga or B) some kind of Amiga
               font simulator, or many of the characters will look weird.

               Desoto of Mo'Soul, to see the colly as it was drawn.)

               on a black background.
