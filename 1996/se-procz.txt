So, It`s been half a year since I last released an asciicollection.
Feels quite funny really, `coz it doesn`t feel like as if it`s been
that long. The reasons are quite a few.

Firstly, I broke up with my girlfriend in the end of january. We`d been
together for nearly two years. So that put a strain on things, of course.

Secondly, I couldn`t concentrate on my studies at the university, so I quit
and did nothing all day. However, i just got a part-time job. Ok, so it`s at
7-eleven, but what the hell, I need money to pay off my telephone-bill. (:

Thirdly, I didn`t feel comfortable in Divine Stylers... Ok, no offence
against dS!, but it just didn`t feel right. I got some offers from
various groups and after some thought, I joined Style. I hope that
I`ll stay for a while, and that everything will work itself out between
me and the other members...

And then it was the matter of getting bored of ascii. I really think that
most fulltime ascii-artists go through one (atleast) of these periods during
their careers. Since november I`ve been losing motivation, and my amount of
logos has decreased. In January-February I was down to one logo/day, and in
march-may i went down to approx. 2 logos/week. Nowadays I make maybe one
logo/week. Now and then I get these energykicks and make four logos in a row,
but that`s hardly productive, is it? (:  Now I`m sitting here with
over 150 logos on my harddrive, and I`m thinking of how to release them.
To release all of them in this collection would be dead boo-ring, and
would result in a collection approx 400kb long. Yawn etc... So I`ve
decided to release several collecions shortly. This one, being the
first, consists only of requests. The next two ones will be a tribute
collection and a file_id.diz ^ bbs collection.

If you have requested a logo from me and it`s not in here, it`s probably
in one of the ones to come. If you are an asciiartist, it`ll probably be
in my tribute collection. Seeing that this collectiom has about 80 logos
in it, you can see that I`ve managed to spread out the logos quite well.
The forthcoming collections will be quite short (or atleast, shortER (: )
so you won`t have to spend too long time reading my BS. (Not that I
think there are very many of you reading this either...)

Oh yeah, there`s another thing too. Throughout these last couple of months
I`ve managed to aquire a few enemies. Mostly newcomers to the scene.
Spreading rumours like that i was kicked out of euphoria (i left them)...
I`ve beem around for a while, and I`ve come to dislike newcomers that
think that they own the scene just `coz they`ve just got themselves
an account on an elite bbs and uploaded 30mb a week. I`m talking
no experience, no etiquette, no respect, no humbleness and no brains.
People that get pissed off with me `coz I nuke their 2 week old warez.
`Coz I delete their new user accounts on bbs`s that are way over
their league. `Coz I answer "No" or not at all to their unpersonal
requests though orderdoors.

The latter is something that I hate. But then people say "But why are you
in the orderdoor if you won`t do any requests that are placed there?".
Well, let me tell you:
The only reason I`m in the door, is so that people that want a logo
from me will see that I`m on that bbs. If they then want a logo all
they have to do is to write me a nice letter, telling me what kind
of logo they want and what the are going to use it for. A way of
getting logos even faster (thus zooming by my "yawn, I`ll do it later"
list) is to tell me why I`m asked to do the specific logo. It`s as
easy as that. So now I have loads of newbies (not only newbies. There
are even asciiartists that I thought had SOME wit that take my "NO"`s
seriously... sheesh) that think I`m an arrogant stuck-up bastard...
So? See if I care. Get a grip. I think that most serious artists
prefer getting personal requests. I`ve got enough as it is. I spend
about 15 minutes on each logo. Ok, my style is always the same, and
just making the plain logo maybe only takes 5 minutes. But I make
all my logos from scratch, and i spend the rest of the time designing
my logos. So let`s see; approx. 80 logos times 15 minutes is 1200
minutes. That`s 20 hours. Add about 4 hours of designing the colly,
writing this damn text amongst other things, and that`s about 1 day
of ascii. Hence the subname of this collection.

                              PARTY REMEDY -96



FRIDAY:

Ok, so it was time for yet another remedy... Since remedy 95
totally and utterly sucked, I nearly didn`t go, but I
thought "what the hell" and made a guy from work fill in for
me (i was meant to work friday, saturday & sunday night).
So, i wandered there at about 15:20 on friday. Met a few
guys like or!on, dmg, herakles, soxius, rooster, java & wraith.
Hung out with them for a while, went to McDonalds and then
took a beer. Went back. Took it easy. Met Nike/Craze that
proclaimed thet he`d seen the swedish "star" ROBYN and a
Motor-TV-Showhost called Crister Glenning. Went to work at 23:30.

SATURDAY:

Worked until 11:00 and went back to the party. Hung around,
played football (Nike/Craze, Vedder and some other guys
(including me ofcoz) were team robyn) against The Black
Lotus. We made the first goal and dominated the first 5
minutes of the match. Unfortunately for us, we didn`t have
any substitutions, which TBL ofcoz had :(. They also had 2
BIIIG finnish guys that acted as walls... Cheating, I`d say!
So, we lost with 1-3. By now I was getting really tired,
having been up for 30 hours in a row. The thing is that I
got up at about 10 am on thursday and went to bed at 04. Got
up at 08 friday and was up ever since. At 17:00 I was ready
for bed and gave my job a call, just to see that everything
was ok. They then told me that the bloke that was going to
work for me suddenly decided that he didn`t want to work for
me on saturday. He wanted to party and said he`d work sunday
instead. After swearing at him on the phone for about 2 hours
i finally agreed to work another night. I went to a friend and
slept from 21-01 after being awake for 37 hours straight.

SUNDAY:

So, I worked yet another night until about 12:00 (getting a
spontaneous nosebleed at 5am, just `coz my body was beginning
to give in to the strain it was being put through) and then
went back to the party. At about 15:00 I decided that i REALLY
needed to sleep... So i slept... .. .  .     .     .        .
Having been awake 67 of the previous 75 hours... I slept
for... 5 hours... amazing eh? After being up for nearly 3
days i slept only 5 hours... And I felt fine... Got up and
listened to the multichannelcompo... Cool remix of the old
c64-classic "commando", but otherwise nothing special.
hung around with some guys like dmg, rooster and wraith...
laughed at some lame guy called "the shrimp" that walked
around asking people if they were elite, and then announcing
that HE was. hahaha... Then we watched the intro/democompo.
TBL won with some dark melancholic thing called darkside.
very professional i must say. And c-lous came second with a
very well coded demo called Colour Remix. Then it was time
for the wildcompo. Since i just joined up with the group
"oss poetrikers samvete" i joined in on the wildcompo.
Vedder, Nike, rooster, Blomsterleif, another guy and I sat
on the stage, drinking beer and playing Poker. this went on
for about 5 minuted until people got bored and started
shouting at us... so we went down... Cool. Skutt/LED was a
bit pissed tho :) haha... Nothing much happened. Watched
Räk-Klubbens first release, an animation of The Shrimp
dancing and asking "are you elite?"... Cool. Made them a
file_id.diz and helped them spread it... (:

MONDAY:

dMG and i ran around and collected empty coke-bottles.
At about 08:00 on monday morning he and i went to the
shopping mall and recycled the bottles. We got a whopping
310 SEK to share, so we went to McDonalds that was due to
open in 30 minutes. There we hooked up with Killermachine
and Cyberwolf. Citruz came by and announced that he was
going home. And Heinrich came by too and kept up company.
When we came back to the partyplace it was nearly 10:00 and
there was hardly anyone left. So dMG and i left. Came home
at 12:00 and watched tv until 14:00. Decided to go to bed...
Realized that during the __ _ 100 _ __ hours that had gone
by, I`d slept _ 13 _ and worked _ 20 _   . ...  gosh ... .
