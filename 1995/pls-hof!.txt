          So, now it's time again. Another ascii-collection made
          by me has come to your computer. This time a bit longer
          and bigger than usual, and that's because I've put two
          collections together, just to please all of you who
          thought it was boring to just scan through 15 logos.
          To all of you, here's 31 brand new logos to admire
          and use as a guide when making your own asciis. Here
          you can find 3d-logos fixed with plain ones in a very
          tasteful combination. Feel free to use them, but always
          remember to include the [cRL]-sign, or I'll put an end
          to your career.
          - -------- -------- - - - -- - -- - -- -------- ------ --
           When speaking about guides, it has come to my knowledge
          that my previous collection 'dOTTIES pLACE' didn't work
          on all computers. I do appologize, but since it worked
          on my A600, and a friends A500, I thought it was allright
          to release it. I became quite embarrassed when [rECALL]
          told me it didn't work. But to all you who thought I
          cheated on you, I just want to say, that it was probably
          the first time ever one released a ascii-collection in
          .guide-format, and I wouldn't be the first to claim that
          I managed it. without visable proves. But somehow I failed,
          and I feel quite guilty about it. Therefor I beg all you
          sysops with the file 'pls-dott.lha' not to delete it, nor
          the bug-fixed collection vol 1.1. ok?
          - -------- -------- - - - -- - -- - -- -------- ------ --
           To all you Swedish users who needs some ascii to your
          system; why don't you join [c-REAL!]-fan club. Just
          read the Swedish note in the end of this collection. If
          you are a forigner, but still want some logos, please
          mail me at one of my home-systems, also named in the end,
          or send me a letter.
          - -------- -------- - - - -- - -- - -- -------- ------ --
          So, back to the introduction of this colly called 'hALL
          oF fAME'. Well, soon, I'm there. With that many requests
          I get every day, I do think that someone working with a
          certain magazine soon will notice my work.
          - -------- -------- - - - -- - -- - -- -------- ------ --
          Ok, time to hit it, but remember, there's much
          unsaid, and even more undone.....
