                     Hiya! And HAPPY NEW YEAR 1996!!!

   You  thought I was dead, right? You were reliefed because  I didn't
   release anything in a long time? But here I am with the same krappy
   style  etc. Somebody  might actually like it, but not me. :)  Well,
   I lost  my request-file,  so I had to wait for new requests, that's
   the  reason  for  the delay and small size. Some people  wanted  me
   to  revive  my old style, because they liked it.  A miracle?  Well,
   IF you like my style, PLEASE order something. I'm sick of releasing
                           these   50k   collys.

   As  usual the compos were late (no complaints) but they were  badly
   organized.  The gfx was showed 5-10 seconds per picture.  But there
   was   something  good  too.  Mild  alcohol  drinks  were   allowed!
   Well,   the  amiga-people  were  the  only ones  that  were  drunk.
   Read the party-report by Slice/nvx^abs for more info.  I think I'll
   go have a few beers. Cya!
