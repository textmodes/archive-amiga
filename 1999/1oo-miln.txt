    Hi folks ... this could've been also named "Back to the future", but
    guess why this collection is called "Millennium" !

    Actually I chose this name because of two reasons:

    1. The 3rd Millennium is coming! Hey....! Come on, didn't you
       book a Millennium-lasting trip somewhere far away, spending all
       your money, just to see January the 1st - first ? Haven't you
       spend *the* night to "produce" your Millennium-baby with your partner,
       just to get famous and rich?
       If not so, I welcome you among the few sane people on this planet :)

    2. It was nearly a Millennium ago that I released my last collection
       "The Sweet Course" (X-Mas '97 !!) and a lot of things have changed
       since then. The amiga-scene is still fading - the BBSing also does,
       and the internet is growing every day, the flavour is gone.
       and.... I can't believe... I replaced my lovely A12oo with a
       a goddamn AMD K6/2. It was a really hard step, and I'm longing for my
       good ol' a12oo - but I need it for my studies.
       The editors are all crap, nothing reaches CygnusEd :)
       But I'm still feeling like I'm a part of the Amiga-community, and
       I guess this will never change, because the Amiga was a part of my
       life for many years.

     Ok, enough, before I start crying.... nahhh.. ;)

     The collection contains just a few (not requested) logos for my pals
     in the 1oo%-clique, my guest-artists and some other stuff. It's just to
     show you, that the 1oo%-family isn't dead at all (the 3rd collection
     within 1 month, ha! ;)

     And I hope you feel invited to write email and request some logos..
     see "Millennium" as a kind of advertising :)

     I won't bother you with endless lyrics from any song, that moves me...

     Bye for now, hope to read you soon
                                                      Miracle/1oo% (13/May/99)

                                                       <mrcl@01019freenet.de>
