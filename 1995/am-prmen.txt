              Welcome to my first "official" ASCII collection!
        This was released  due to the number  of people starting up
      fly-by-night  BBS's and  needing title  banners for  just about
     everything. So hope you like them, they're drawn in my "readable"
     style (you hear  that ASCII/ANSI artists!  They're supposed to be
     readable!) so even the lamest of your BBS members will be able to
     deceifer them. These were done mainly for Amiga owners running /X
     or Xenolink,  so most of  the more common  titles required should
      be here. Well that's just about the end of the intro, enjoy the
        logos! They've taken about 3 weeks on-and-off but are worth
            it! See below for all the greets! On with the show!
